❓ FAQ

Is Iron Man good for beginners?

Yes — his kit is straightforward and his range keeps him safe. Focus on positioning above the fight.

When should I use Unibeam?

Always charge it fully before releasing. Use it on tanky targets or grouped enemies for maximum value.

How do I survive dive assassins?

Keep Rocket Boost off cooldown. The moment a diver closes in, boost upward and reset the engagement.
