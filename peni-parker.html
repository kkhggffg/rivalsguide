Peni Parker
Complete Guide

The SP//dr pilot — who dominates objectives through cyber-web mine fields, slows and immobilizes advancing enemies with her mech's web attacks, and creates impenetrable area denial zones that force entire teams to rethink their approach.

If you want a Vanguard who wins games through map control rather than raw HP — a tank who can make entire sections of the map unplayable for the enemy team while simultaneously healing herself and her allies — Peni Parker is the most unique and frustrating-to-fight Vanguard in Marvel Rivals.

Peni Parker is A-Tier and one of the most uniquely powerful Vanguards in Marvel Rivals. Her 650 HP pool is among the highest in the roster, and her mine-based area denial is the single best objective control tool available to any tank. She dominates static defense scenarios — payload defense, capture point holds, and chokepoint control — but struggles significantly against aerial compositions and in open-field engagements where she cannot establish her mine network. Kit Breakdown

Abilities & Skills

PASSIVE
Cyber-Weave
Peni Parker's SP//dr mech passively generates cyber-web material over time, which is consumed by her Arachno-Mine ability. When mines are detonated by enemies, the explosion heals Peni and nearby allies within the blast radius. This passive healing mechanic makes her uniquely self-sustaining among Vanguards — a fully established mine network essentially provides continuous area healing whenever enemies push through it. [[1]](#__1)
💡 The passive healing from mine detonations is most powerful on objective points where enemies must repeatedly walk through your mine field. Each detonation heals you and nearby allies — a sustained push through a fully mined objective can heal your entire team significantly over the course of a fight. [[1]](#__1)
LMB
SP//dr Fist
Peni's SP//dr mech delivers a powerful melee strike dealing 50 damage per hit. As a melee primary attack, it requires close range — but the SP//dr mech's 650 HP pool means she can sustain close-range engagements far longer than any other Duelist or Support. The primary attack also generates cyber-web material for mine deployment, incentivizing active combat rather than passive mine camping. [[1]](#__1) [[3]](#__3)
Damage: 50 Type: Melee Range: Close
💡 Use SP//dr Fist to generate cyber-web material between mine deployments. Staying actively engaged in melee combat accelerates your mine cooldown recharge, letting you maintain a denser mine network throughout the fight. [[1]](#__1)
RMB
Cyber-Web Snare
Peni fires a web projectile that slows any enemy it hits by 30% for 2 seconds. The slow makes it significantly harder for enemies to escape her mine field, dodge follow-up attacks, or reposition away from her team's focus fire. Against dive heroes attempting to close the gap, the 30% slow provides just enough time for her team to respond before the diver reaches the backline. [[1]](#__1) [[3]](#__3)
Slow: 30% Duration: 2s Type: Projectile Cooldown: 8s
💡 Use Cyber-Web Snare on enemies entering your mine field — the 30% slow forces them to spend more time inside the mine zone, dramatically increasing the chance of triggering multiple mines. A slowed enemy walking through a 3-mine field is almost guaranteed to detonate at least two of them. [[1]](#__1)
E
Arachno-Mine
Peni deploys a cyber-web mine on any surface that detonates when an enemy comes within proximity, dealing 80 damage in an AoE and healing Peni and nearby allies. She can have up to 3 mines active simultaneously. Mines can be placed on walls, ceilings, floors, and any surface — making them extremely versatile for creating layered trap networks. Each mine has its own HP and can be destroyed by enemy AoE before detonating. [[1]](#__1) [[3]](#__3)
Damage: 80 per mine Max active: 3 Placement: Any surface Heal: On detonation
💡 Layer mines at different heights — one on the floor, one on a wall at waist height, one on the ceiling. Enemies clearing the floor mine with AoE often miss the wall and ceiling mines entirely. A 3-mine layered setup deals 240 damage to any enemy who triggers all three, eliminating most non-tank heroes outright. [[0]](#__0) [[1]](#__1)
SHIFT
Web Tangle
Peni fires a web that immobilizes the first enemy it hits for 1.5 seconds, completely preventing all movement. Unlike a slow, immobilization stops all movement abilities — dive heroes cannot dash out, flying heroes are grounded, and mobile Duelists cannot use their repositioning tools. The 1.5-second window is enough for her entire team to focus fire on the immobilized target. [[1]](#__1) [[3]](#__3)
Immobilize: 1.5s Type: Hard CC Cooldown: 12s
💡 Web Tangle is your highest-value ability in coordinated play — communicate with your team before using it. An immobilized target that your team immediately focuses is a guaranteed elimination. Use it on the highest-priority target: the enemy carry, the diving hero, or the support trying to escape. [[0]](#__0) [[1]](#__1)
Q — ULT
Arachno-Burst
Peni activates her SP//dr mech's full power, rapidly deploying a massive web network across a large area that simultaneously detonates all active mines and deploys new ones in a wide radius. The ult creates an instant mine field covering the entire objective area, dealing massive AoE damage to all enemies caught in the detonation zone while healing Peni and all nearby allies. The combination of instant mine detonation plus new mine deployment makes it both an offensive burst and a defensive area denial tool simultaneously. [[1]](#__1) [[3]](#__3)
Type: AoE burst + area denial Effect: Detonates all mines Heal: Self + allies
💡 Activate Arachno-Burst when enemies are grouped on the objective AND you have all 3 mines active. The simultaneous detonation of 3 mines deals 240 damage to every enemy in the zone — enough to eliminate most supports and Duelists outright. The new mine deployment immediately re-establishes area denial after the burst. [[1]](#__1) [[2]](#__2)
TEAM-UP
Spider-Clan — with Spider-Man & Venom
When paired with Spider-Man and/or Venom, Peni gains access to the Spider-Clan team-up ability. This synergy enhances her web-based abilities and grants additional effects tied to the Spider-Clan network. Spider-Man's mobility complements Peni's area denial — while she locks down the objective, Spider-Man applies pressure to the enemy backline, forcing enemies to choose between contesting the objective or dealing with the flanker. [[1]](#__1) [[3]](#__3)
💡 The Spider-Clan composition is uniquely effective because Peni and Spider-Man cover completely opposite playstyles — Peni is the slowest, most stationary Vanguard while Spider-Man is the fastest, most mobile Duelist. Together they force enemies to split attention between two completely different threats simultaneously. [[0]](#__0) [[1]](#__1)

Mine Network Setup

  • 1
    Arrive at the objective 5–8 seconds before the fight begins pre-fight
    Peni needs setup time — arriving early to establish her mine network is the single most important habit to develop. A fully mined objective before the enemy team arrives is dramatically more effective than deploying mines reactively during the fight. [[1]](#__1)
  • 2
    Deploy first mine at the primary approach angle E 0.0s
    Place the first mine at the most likely enemy approach route — the main entrance to the objective, the chokepoint, or the doorway. This mine will trigger first and deal the initial 80 damage to the lead enemy. [[1]](#__1) [[3]](#__3)
  • 3
    Deploy second and third mines at different heights on walls E × 2
    Place the remaining two mines on walls at waist and head height rather than the floor. Enemies who clear the floor mine with AoE will miss the wall mines entirely. Three mines at different heights covering the same approach route deal 240 damage to any enemy who triggers all three. [[0]](#__0) [[1]](#__1)
  • 4
    Immediately redeploy destroyed mines to new positions E
    When enemies destroy your mines with AoE, never place replacements in the same positions. Move to a different wall, a different height, or a secondary approach route. Enemies who just cleared your mine field will look in the same locations for replacements. [[0]](#__0) [[1]](#__1)
Pro tip: The mine network is most effective when enemies cannot see all three mines from a single vantage point. If an enemy can spot and destroy all 3 mines without moving, your placement is too obvious. The ideal setup forces enemies to clear mines one at a time from different positions — giving you time to redeploy between each clear. [[0]](#__0) [[1]](#__1)

Snare + Mine Funnel

  • 1
    Establish a dense mine field at the objective chokepoint E × 3 pre-fight
    The Snare + Mine Funnel combo requires a pre-established mine field to be effective. Deploy all 3 mines at the chokepoint before the enemy team arrives — the combo only works if mines are already in place when you land the snare. [[1]](#__1)
  • 2
    Hit the lead enemy with Cyber-Web Snare as they approach RMB 0.0s
    Fire Cyber-Web Snare at the first enemy entering the chokepoint. The 30% slow forces them to spend significantly more time inside the mine zone — a slowed enemy walking through a 3-mine field is almost guaranteed to trigger all three mines before the slow expires. [[1]](#__1) [[3]](#__3)
  • 3
    Follow up with SP//dr Fist melee while they're slowed LMB
    While the enemy is slowed inside the mine field, close the gap and add SP//dr Fist melee damage. The combined mine detonations plus melee hits while slowed creates a burst window that most non-tank heroes cannot survive. [[1]](#__1)
  • 4
    Immediately redeploy mines to maintain the field E
    After the slowed enemy triggers your mines, immediately redeploy replacements before the next enemy enters the chokepoint. The goal is to maintain a continuously active mine field — never let the chokepoint go unguarded for more than a few seconds. [[1]](#__1) [[3]](#__3)
Why it works: Cyber-Web Snare transforms Peni's static mine field into an active kill zone. Without the slow, mobile enemies can sometimes sprint through the mine field quickly enough to only trigger one or two mines. The 30% slow ensures they spend enough time in the zone to trigger all three — turning a 80–160 damage encounter into a guaranteed 240 damage hit. [[0]](#__0) [[1]](#__1)

Web Tangle Kill Setup

  • 1
    Identify the highest-priority enemy target pre-engage
    Web Tangle's 1.5-second immobilize is your highest-value CC ability — save it for the enemy carry, the diving hero, or the support trying to escape. Using it on a Vanguard wastes the CC on a target your team cannot burst down in 1.5 seconds. [[1]](#__1)
  • 2
    Communicate the Web Tangle target to your team 0.5s before
    Call out "Web Tangle on [target]" before activating. The 1.5-second window is only enough to secure a kill if your entire team immediately focuses the immobilized target — an uncoordinated Web Tangle wastes the CC entirely. [[0]](#__0) [[1]](#__1)
  • 3
    Land Web Tangle on the priority target SHIFT 0.0s
    Fire Web Tangle directly at the priority target. The immobilize stops all movement — dive heroes cannot dash out, flying heroes are grounded, mobile Duelists cannot use repositioning tools. The target is completely stationary for 1.5 seconds. [[1]](#__1) [[3]](#__3)
  • 4
    Immediately follow with SP//dr Fist + mine detonation LMB + E
    While the target is immobilized, deploy a mine directly on their position and immediately melee them. The mine detonates instantly on contact, dealing 80 damage while your team focuses fire. A Web Tangle into mine + melee + team focus fire eliminates most heroes before the 1.5 seconds expires. [[0]](#__0) [[1]](#__1)
Why it works: Web Tangle's hard immobilize is uniquely powerful because it stops movement abilities — not just movement speed. Spider-Man cannot web-swing out, Black Panther cannot dash, Psylocke cannot blink. The 1.5-second window where a mobile hero is completely stationary is enough for coordinated team focus fire to eliminate any target in the game. [[0]](#__0) [[1]](#__1) [[2]](#__2)

Arachno-Burst Team Wipe Expert

Maximizing Arachno-Burst damage by pre-loading all 3 mines before activation for a simultaneous 240-damage detonation.

  • 1
    Confirm all 3 mines are active on the objective before activating ult pre-ult
    Arachno-Burst detonates all active mines simultaneously — if you have 0 or 1 mines active when you ult, the damage output is dramatically reduced. Always wait until all 3 mines are deployed before activating. [[1]](#__1)
  • 2
    Wait for enemies to group on the objective timing
    The ult's AoE detonation hits every enemy in the mine zone simultaneously. Wait for the enemy team to commit to contesting the objective — a full team grouped on the point triggers all 3 mines for 240 damage to every hero in the zone. [[1]](#__1) [[3]](#__3)
  • 3
    Activate Arachno-Burst at peak enemy grouping Q 0.0s
    Activate the ult the moment the maximum number of enemies are inside the mine zone. The simultaneous detonation of all 3 mines deals 240 damage to every enemy in the zone — eliminating most supports and Duelists outright and severely chipping Vanguards. [[1]](#__1)
  • 4
    Immediately Web Tangle the lowest HP survivor SHIFT
    After the burst, identify the lowest HP enemy who survived. Immediately Web Tangle them — they're at critically low HP and immobilized, making them a guaranteed elimination for your team's follow-up. The new mine deployment from the ult re-establishes area denial simultaneously. [[0]](#__0) [[1]](#__1)
Why it works: Arachno-Burst's simultaneous mine detonation is Peni's highest single-rotation damage output. Three mines detonating at once deal 240 damage to every enemy in the zone — no other Vanguard ultimate deals this much damage to grouped enemies. The immediate Web Tangle follow-up converts the burst into a guaranteed elimination, and the new mine deployment means the objective is instantly re-fortified after the wipe.
