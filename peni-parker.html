Peni Parker

🛡️ Tank

Pilot of the SP//dr mech suit. Peni Parker is a zone-control Tank who dominates enclosed spaces with her web mines, making her one of the strongest defenders on objective-based maps. [[0]](#__0)

Peni has one of the highest health pools in the game. Her strength comes not from raw damage but from area denial — forcing enemies to fight on her terms. [[1]](#__1)

⚡ Abilities

🔷 SP//dr Fist (Primary Fire)

Fires energy blasts from the SP//dr mech at medium range. Consistent damage output that works best when enemies are slowed or caught in mines. Use this to finish off enemies who are already trapped. [[1]](#__1)

🕸️ Arachno-Mine (Shift)

Places a web mine on a surface that detonates when enemies walk near it, dealing damage and slowing them. This is Peni's core ability — mines placed in doorways, on objectives, and around corners create a deadly zone that enemies must navigate carefully. [[0]](#__0)

🛡️ Cyber-Web Barrier (E)

Deploys a web barrier that blocks enemy movement and projectiles for a short duration. Use to cut off flanking routes, protect teammates, or block a key chokepoint during a push. [[1]](#__1)

🤖 SP//dr Rage (Ultimate)

Overcharges the SP//dr suit, dramatically increasing damage output and deploying multiple mines simultaneously across a wide area. Best used when enemies are grouped on the objective — the mine saturation makes the area nearly impassable. [[0]](#__0)

💡 Pro Tips

🕸️ Mine Placement is Everything

Place Arachno-Mines at head height on doorframes and corners — enemies often don't look up and will walk straight into them. Layering 2–3 mines in the same chokepoint creates a near-instant kill zone. [[0]](#__0)

📍 Own the Objective

Peni is at her strongest when she has time to set up mines around the objective before enemies arrive. Rotate early, place mines, then hold position. Enemies who push in will take massive chip damage before the fight even starts. [[1]](#__1)

📏 Distance is Your Enemy's Friend

Long-range heroes like Hawkeye and Black Widow can safely destroy your mines and poke you down without ever entering your mine field. When facing snipers, reposition aggressively and close the gap. [[3]](#__3)

🤖 Ultimate Combo

Activate SP//dr Rage right as enemies commit to pushing the objective. The sudden mine saturation combined with your boosted damage output can wipe an entire team in seconds. Coordinate with your support to stay alive through the channel. [[2]](#__2)

⚔️ Counters Peni Parker

The key to countering Peni is staying at distance and destroying her mines before engaging. Never fight her in enclosed spaces on her terms. [[1]](#__1)
