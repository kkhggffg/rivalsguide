Black Widow

A nimble assassin and sniper who operates effectively at both close and long range. High skill ceiling with massive reward for precise aim. [[0]](#__0)

⚡ Abilities

🎯 Widow's Bite Rifle (Primary)

Precision sniper rifle with scope. Headshots deal massive damage. Switch between scoped and hip-fire modes for different ranges. [[2]](#__2)

🕸️ Grapple Hook (Shift)

Launches a grapple to quickly reposition. Essential for escaping danger and reaching high-ground sniping positions.

⚡ Electric Baton (E)

Close-range melee combo that deals significant damage. Allows her to switch between sniper and melee playstyles fluidly. [[2]](#__2)

🎯 Electro-Web (Ultimate)

Fires a web that roots and damages enemies in an area. Great for locking down key targets or peeling for allies.

💡 Pro Tips

🎯 Aim for Headshots

Practice in the range daily with moving targets. Black Widow's value is almost entirely tied to headshot consistency — body shots are too low damage. [[0]](#__0)

📍 High Ground Control

Use Grapple Hook to claim elevated positions. From high ground, you can snipe supports and DPS freely while being hard to reach. [[2]](#__2)

🔄 Melee Hybrid

Against flankers like Loki or Mantis, switch to melee baton mode. She can tag an ulting Mantis or Luna to reduce healing output significantly. [[3]](#__3)
