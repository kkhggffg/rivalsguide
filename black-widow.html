⚡ Abilities

🎯 Widow's Bite Rifle (Primary)

Precision sniper rifle with scope. Headshots deal massive damage. Switch between scoped and hip-fire modes for different engagement ranges.

🕸️ Grapple Hook (Shift)

Launches a grapple to quickly reposition. Essential for escaping danger and reaching high-ground sniping positions before enemies can react.

⚡ Electric Baton (E)

Close-range melee combo that deals significant damage. Allows her to switch between sniper and melee playstyles fluidly depending on the situation.

🎯 Electro-Web (Ultimate)

Fires a web that roots and damages enemies in an area. Great for locking down key targets or peeling for allies being dived.

💡 Pro Tips

🎯 Aim for Headshots Always

Practice in the range daily with moving targets. Black Widow's value is almost entirely tied to headshot consistency — body shots deal too little damage to be effective.

📍 High Ground Control

Use Grapple Hook to claim elevated positions at the start of every fight. From high ground, you can snipe supports and DPS freely while being hard to reach.

🔄 Melee Hybrid Playstyle

Against flankers who get close, switch to Electric Baton mode immediately. She can deal surprising burst damage in melee range and catch enemies off guard.

🎯 Ultimate on Supports

Use Electro-Web to root the enemy support during a team fight. A rooted Mantis or Loki cannot escape and becomes an easy kill for your team.
