Mantis

💚 Support S-Tier

The empathic powerhouse of Marvel Rivals. Mantis is a top-tier Strategist who can heal allies, boost their damage output, and even put enemies to sleep — making her one of the most versatile supports in the game. [[0]](#__0)

Mantis has low health but compensates with exceptional utility — she can heal, buff damage, and disable enemies all in one kit. [[1]](#__1)

⚡ Abilities

🌸 Empathic Pulse (Primary Fire)

Fires a burst of empathic energy that heals allies or damages enemies on hit. Switching between heal and damage modes is key to maximizing her value in fights. Use on allies when they're taking damage, switch to enemies when your team is safe. [[1]](#__1)

💫 Life Orb (Shift)

Launches a healing orb that attaches to an ally and provides continuous healing over time (HoT). Prioritize attaching this to your tank or the ally under the most pressure. The orb persists even if you switch targets, giving you freedom to heal others simultaneously. [[2]](#__2)

😴 Spore Slumber (E)

Releases empathic spores that put enemies to sleep, rendering them unable to act for a short duration. This is one of the strongest crowd-control abilities in the game — use it to interrupt enemy ultimates or shut down a flanker diving your backline. [[1]](#__1)

🌟 Allied Inspiration (Passive)

When Mantis heals an ally, she also temporarily boosts their damage output. This passive makes her uniquely powerful — she doesn't just keep allies alive, she makes them hit harder too. [[0]](#__0)

🌺 Natural Restoration (Ultimate)

Channels a powerful burst of empathic energy that heals all nearby allies for a large amount and boosts their damage simultaneously. Best used when your team is grouped on the objective and taking heavy fire — it can single-handedly turn a lost fight. [[2]](#__2)

💡 Pro Tips

🎯 Heal AND Damage Simultaneously

Mantis's passive means every heal also buffs ally damage. Don't just spam heals reactively — proactively heal your DPS right before they engage to give them the damage boost when it matters most. [[1]](#__1)

😴 Sleep = Ultimate Counter

Spore Slumber is one of the best anti-ultimate tools in the game. Save it specifically to interrupt high-value ultimates like Thanos's charge or Thor's lightning. A well-timed sleep can negate an entire enemy ultimate and swing the fight completely. [[2]](#__2)

📍 Positioning: Mid-Range is Ideal

Mantis is not a backline healer — she needs to be at medium range to keep her Life Orb on tanks and still reach DPS allies. Stay behind your tank but don't hide in the back. Visibility of your whole team is key. [[3]](#__3)

🌺 Ultimate Timing

Don't use Natural Restoration when your team is scattered. Wait until your team is grouped on the point or pushing together — the simultaneous heal + damage buff can enable a full wipe. [[2]](#__2)

⚠️ Watch for Black Widow

Black Widow can tag you mid-ultimate to reduce your healing output significantly. Stay aware of enemy snipers and use cover when channeling your ultimate. [[3]](#__3)

⚔️ Counters Mantis

Mantis's low health makes her vulnerable to dive and sniper pressure. Apply constant pressure so she never feels comfortable. [[3]](#__3)
