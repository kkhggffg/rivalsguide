Mantis
Complete Guide

The Celestial Madonna — who heals allies through empathic orbs, amplifies her team's damage output with a unique boost mechanic, puts dangerous enemies to sleep with a single touch, and revives fallen teammates with her ultimate.

If you want a Strategist who does more than just heal — a support who can single-handedly double a Duelist's kill rate through damage boost, shut down the most dangerous enemy with a sleep, and swing entire teamfights with a well-timed revive ultimate — Mantis is the highest-impact Strategist in Marvel Rivals.

Mantis is S-Tier and the most impactful Strategist in Marvel Rivals. Her damage boost ability is uniquely powerful — a 25% damage amplifier on a Hela or Moon Knight effectively turns a two-tap into a one-tap against most targets. Her sleep CC is the best single-target incapacitation in the support roster, and her revive ultimate is the highest single-ult team recovery tool available. The skill expression is in target prioritization: knowing which ally to boost, when to sleep, and when to save the ultimate for maximum impact.

Kit Breakdown

Abilities & Skills

PASSIVE
Empathic Sensing
Mantis passively senses the emotional state of nearby allies, granting her enhanced awareness of ally HP levels even through walls and terrain. This passive allows her to prioritize healing targets more effectively than other Strategists — she always knows which ally is lowest HP without needing line of sight, letting her pre-position for healing before the ally takes critical damage.
💡 Use Empathic Sensing to pre-position for healing before your lowest HP ally takes critical damage. Mantis's healing range is short — if you wait until you can see the ally taking damage, you're already too late to prevent their death. Passive HP awareness lets you move toward them proactively.
LMB
Healing Spores
Mantis fires empathic healing orbs at a target ally, restoring 60 HP per second while the orbs are active. The healing is continuous as long as she maintains the beam on the target. Unlike burst heals, the continuous nature means she must stay focused on a single target — switching targets mid-heal wastes the ramp-up time. The orbs have a moderate range of approximately 20 meters.
Healing: 60 HP/s Type: Continuous beam Range: ~20m Ammo: Infinite
💡 Prioritize healing your most aggressive Duelist — the carry who is dealing the most damage is the most valuable target to keep alive. A Hela or Moon Knight at full HP deals more value than a Vanguard at full HP. Learn to track your carry's HP passively and keep the healing beam on them during engagements.
RMB
Life Drain
Mantis fires a damaging projectile that deals 45 damage to enemies and heals herself for 45 HP on hit. This is her primary self-sustain tool and her only offensive damage ability. When dived by a melee hero, Life Drain allows her to trade HP effectively — each hit heals her while damaging the attacker. Against a single diver, sustained Life Drain hits can keep her alive long enough for teammates to respond.
Damage: 45 Self-heal: 45 HP per hit Type: Projectile Cooldown: None
💡 When dived, immediately switch from Healing Spores to Life Drain and aim at the diver's body. The 45 HP self-heal per hit means you're effectively gaining HP while dealing damage — a sustained Life Drain exchange against a single diver gives your team time to peel. Never panic and run without firing back.
E
Damage Boost
Mantis empowers a single target ally with a 25% damage amplifier for 4 seconds. During the boost window, every damage instance the ally deals is multiplied by 1.25 — including primary fire, abilities, and ultimate damage. This is the highest single-target damage amplifier in the Strategist roster. The 8-second cooldown means it can be applied to a different ally every 8 seconds, creating a rotation of boosted carries throughout a fight.
Boost: +25% damage Duration: 4s Cooldown: 8s Target: Single ally
💡 Always boost your highest-damage Duelist — Hela, Moon Knight, or Winter Soldier. A 25% boost on Hela turns her two-tap (280 damage) into a one-tap against 250 HP targets (350 damage). Never boost a Vanguard or a Strategist — the damage amplifier only generates value on heroes who are actively dealing high damage.
SHIFT
Sleep Spore
Mantis fires a sleep projectile that puts the first enemy it hits into a complete sleep state for 2 seconds. While sleeping, the target cannot move, attack, or use abilities. The sleep breaks immediately if the sleeping target takes any damage — making communication with teammates essential. The 12-second cooldown means it must be used deliberately on the highest-value target available.
Sleep duration: 2s Type: Hard CC projectile Cooldown: 12s Break condition: Any damage
💡 Call out "sleeping [target]" immediately before firing Sleep Spore — teammates must hold their fire for the full 2 seconds to maximize the CC value. The highest-value sleep targets are: the enemy carry Duelist mid-ultimate, a diving hero mid-approach, or the enemy Strategist trying to activate their own ultimate. A slept enemy ult is completely wasted.
Q — ULT
Natural Order
Mantis channels her full empathic power to simultaneously revive up to 3 fallen allies within a large radius, restoring them to partial HP. The revived allies return to the fight immediately at the location they died, with enough HP to contribute to the ongoing teamfight. Natural Order is the highest single-ult team recovery tool in Marvel Rivals — a 3-ally revive during a losing teamfight can instantly flip the engagement from a loss to a win.
Max revives: 3 allies Type: AoE revive HP on revive: Partial
💡 Save Natural Order for teamfight recovery, not preemptive use. The ult generates maximum value when 2–3 allies have just fallen — activating it immediately after losing a teamfight revives them at their death positions, which are often already inside the objective. Never use it when only 1 ally is down; the cooldown cost is too high for a single revive.
TEAM-UP
Florakinesis — with Star-Lord & Adam Warlock
When paired with Star-Lord and/or Adam Warlock, Mantis gains access to the Florakinesis team-up ability — enhancing her empathic healing with additional nature energy effects. The team-up amplifies her Healing Spores output and grants additional effects tied to cosmic empathic power. Running the full Guardians of the Galaxy composition with Star-Lord, Adam Warlock, and Mantis creates a uniquely synergistic team with enhanced healing, revive, and damage boost coverage.
💡 The Guardians composition works best when Mantis focuses her Damage Boost on Star-Lord — his rapid-fire primary fire benefits enormously from the 25% amplifier, and Adam Warlock's revive passive provides a safety net that lets Mantis play more aggressively with her Sleep Spore and Life Drain.
Matchup Analysis

Counters & Matchups

✅ Mantis Counters
💥
Enemy Ultimate Users
Sleep Spore fired at an enemy activating their ultimate completely cancels the ult — a slept Hela cannot fire Goddess of Death, a slept Moon Knight cannot activate Hand of Khonshu. The 2-second sleep window wastes the entire ult cooldown.
🛡️
Single-Target Divers
Life Drain self-heal lets Mantis trade HP effectively against a single diver — each hit heals her while damaging the attacker. A sustained Life Drain exchange against one diver gives teammates time to peel.
🔫
Damage-Dependent Carries
Damage Boost amplifies every damage-dependent carry in the game. Heroes whose power is entirely in raw damage output — Hela, Moon Knight, Winter Soldier — are dramatically more lethal with Mantis's 25% boost active.
🔄
Teams Relying on Attrition
Natural Order revive completely resets attrition-based victories — teams who win by slowly eliminating enemies one by one are countered by a 3-ally revive that instantly restores the numbers advantage.
❌ Hard Counters Mantis
🕷️
Multi-Diver Compositions
Life Drain handles one diver effectively — two simultaneous divers overwhelm her self-sustain. Spider-Man + Black Panther diving simultaneously eliminates her before Life Drain can compensate for the combined burst.
🔇
Anti-Heal Heroes (Scarlet Witch)
Abilities that reduce healing effectiveness directly counter Mantis's Healing Spores output — her 60 HP/s healing rate becomes significantly less effective when healing reduction is applied to her targets.
💨
High-Burst One-Shot Compositions
At 250 HP, Mantis is eliminated by any one-shot burst combo before she can activate Life Drain. Compositions built around instant-burst elimination — Hela headshots, Punisher ult — bypass her self-sustain entirely.
🎯
Long-Range Snipers (Hela, Hawkeye)
At 250 HP, Hela's two-tap eliminates Mantis before she can respond. Long-range heroes can target her safely from outside her Life Drain effective range, removing her self-sustain advantage entirely.

Boost Rotation Loop

  • 1
    Identify your highest-damage Duelist at the start of each fight pre-fight
    Before every engagement, identify which ally is your primary boost target. It should always be the Duelist with the highest damage potential — Hela, Moon Knight, or Winter Soldier. This target should receive your first Damage Boost of every fight without exception.
  • 2
    Apply Damage Boost to the carry as the fight begins E 0.0s
    Activate Damage Boost on your primary carry the moment the fight starts — before they fire their first shot. The 4-second boost window covers their entire opening burst rotation. A boosted Hela's first two headshots deal 350 damage total, eliminating any 250–275 HP target instantly.
  • 3
    Maintain Healing Spores on the carry during the boost window LMB
    While the boost is active, keep Healing Spores on the same carry. They're the most likely target for enemy focus fire during the boost window — keeping them at full HP ensures they survive long enough to spend the entire 4-second boost dealing damage.
  • 4
    Reapply boost to the same or next carry after the 8s cooldown E
    After the 8-second cooldown, immediately reapply Damage Boost. If your primary carry is dead or out of position, boost the next highest-damage ally. The goal is to have Damage Boost active on someone at all times — a wasted boost cooldown is lost damage that can never be recovered.
Pro tip: The Boost Rotation Loop is Mantis's highest-value contribution in most games. Damage Boost has a 50% uptime (4 seconds active out of every 8-second cooldown) — meaning your carry should be boosted for half of every fight. A Mantis who maintains consistent boost uptime on a Hela will generate more team damage than almost any other support-carry combination in the game.

Sleep + Team Focus

  • 1
  • 2
    Call out the sleep target to your team before firing 0.5s before
    Communication is the entire difference between a wasted sleep and a guaranteed kill. Call "sleeping [target]" in voice or ping them immediately before firing Sleep Spore. Every teammate must hold their fire for the full 2 seconds — a single stray shot breaks the sleep with zero CC value remaining.
  • 3
    Fire Sleep Spore at the priority target SHIFT 0.0s
    Aim Sleep Spore at the highest-priority enemy — the carry Duelist, the enemy Strategist activating their ultimate, or the diving hero mid-approach. The 2-second sleep window completely incapacitates the target, canceling any ability or ultimate they were activating.
  • 4
    Apply Damage Boost to your carry immediately after sleep lands E ~0.2s after sleep
    The moment sleep lands, boost your carry — they now have a stationary, sleeping target to land guaranteed headshots on. A boosted Hela headshot into a sleeping target deals 175 damage in a single shot, and the follow-up second headshot finishes any remaining HP before the 2-second sleep expires. This is the fastest guaranteed kill confirmation in the game.
Why it works: Sleep + Damage Boost is Mantis's highest single-rotation kill setup. The sleep provides a guaranteed stationary target, and the damage boost ensures the first headshot deals enough damage to eliminate most heroes outright. The combination removes the aim difficulty from the equation — a sleeping target cannot dodge, making the boosted headshot a guaranteed hit.

Life Drain Dive Counter

  • 1
    Identify a diving hero approaching your position pre-engage
    Watch for Spider-Man, Black Panther, or Psylocke flanking toward you. The Life Drain counter requires you to react before they reach melee range — if they land their full burst combo before you start firing, the self-heal cannot compensate for the burst damage.
  • 2
    Fire Sleep Spore at the diver mid-approach SHIFT 0.0s
    If Sleep Spore is available, fire it immediately at the approaching diver. A sleeping diver is completely stopped mid-approach — they cannot close the gap, use mobility abilities, or attack for 2 full seconds. This is the cleanest dive counter available to any Strategist in the game.
  • 3
    Switch to Life Drain and aim at the diver's body RMB ongoing
    If Sleep Spore is on cooldown, immediately switch from Healing Spores to Life Drain and fire continuously at the diver. Each hit deals 45 damage and heals you for 45 HP — you're effectively gaining HP while dealing damage. Don't panic and run without firing; sustained Life Drain is your survival tool.
  • 4
    Reposition toward your Vanguard while maintaining Life Drain fire RMB + movement
    While firing Life Drain, move toward your nearest Vanguard or teammate. You don't need to eliminate the diver yourself — you just need to survive long enough for your team to peel. Life Drain self-heal buys the time; movement toward allies closes the distance for peel assistance.
Why it works: Life Drain transforms Mantis from the squishiest Strategist into a surprisingly durable target against single divers. The 45 HP self-heal per hit means a diver who takes 5 Life Drain hits has dealt 225 damage to themselves in return fire while Mantis has healed 225 HP. Against a single diver, this trade is often enough to survive until teammates respond.

Natural Order Teamfight Reset Expert

Using Natural Order at the exact moment that maximizes revive value — turning a losing teamfight into an instant numbers advantage.

  • 1
    Hold Natural Order until 2–3 allies have fallen patience
    Natural Order generates maximum value when 2–3 allies are down simultaneously. Activating it for a single fallen ally wastes the ult's multi-revive potential. The expert skill is holding the ult through the temptation to use it early — wait until the maximum number of allies are down before activating.
  • 2
    Survive long enough to activate using Life Drain and Sleep Spore RMB + SHIFT
    After your team falls, you become the primary target. Use Sleep Spore on the nearest threat to buy time, then sustain with Life Drain while repositioning to a safe location. You must stay alive long enough to activate Natural Order — dying before the ult fires wastes the entire setup.
  • 3
    Activate Natural Order when positioned near the fallen allies Q
    Natural Order revives allies at their death positions — you don't need to be standing on them, but you need to be within the ult's radius. Position yourself near the center of where your allies fell before activating to maximize the number of revives.
  • 4
    Immediately apply Damage Boost to the first revived ally E
    The moment allies are revived, immediately boost your carry Duelist. They've just returned to the fight at partial HP — the damage boost lets them deal maximum damage immediately while your Healing Spores brings them back to full HP. The enemy team just spent their cooldowns killing your allies; the revived team has fresh cooldowns and a damage boost advantage.
Why it works: Natural Order is the highest single-ult teamfight swing in Marvel Rivals. A 3-ally revive during a losing engagement instantly restores the numbers advantage — the enemy team spent their ultimates and cooldowns to eliminate 3 heroes, only to face those same heroes returning to the fight with fresh cooldowns. The immediate Damage Boost follow-up ensures the revived team wins the resulting exchange.

Mantis has a moderate learning curve. Her healing is straightforward, but her Damage Boost requires good target prioritization — boosting the wrong ally significantly reduces her impact. Her Sleep Spore requires team communication to avoid being broken by friendly fire. Players who invest in learning her boost rotation and sleep timing will find her one of the highest-impact supports in the game, but beginners may find the coordination requirements frustrating initially.

If your team has no carry Duelist, boost the most aggressive player on your team regardless of role.

In solo queue without voice, ping the sleeping target with a "hold fire" ping the moment sleep lands. In coordinated play, designate a specific teammate to wait for your sleep call before engaging.
