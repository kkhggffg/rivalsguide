Spider-Man
Complete Guide

Peter Parker — the web-slinging, wall-crawling menace of Marvel Rivals. High mobility, high skill ceiling, devastating in the right hands.

If you want the highest skill ceiling in Marvel Rivals with the most satisfying payoff — Spider-Man is your answer.

DPS S-Tier High Mobility Hard to Master Dive Assassin

Spider-Man is a high-risk, high-reward dive assassin. His 250 HP is among the lowest in the roster, but his unmatched mobility lets him engage, burst, and disengage before enemies can react. Mastering his web movement is the key to unlocking his full potential.

Kit Breakdown

Abilities & Skills

PASSIVE
Wall Crawl
Spider-Man can cling to and traverse any wall or ceiling. This passive fundamentally changes how you approach every fight — always use vertical space.
💡 Use ceilings to approach enemies from unexpected angles. Most players never look up.
LMB
Web Shot
Fires a web projectile dealing 65 damage. Fast projectile speed with moderate range. Your primary damage tool in extended fights.
💡 Aim for the head — headshots significantly increase damage output.
RMB
Web Pull
Fires a web that pulls the target toward Spider-Man. Can interrupt enemy abilities mid-animation. One of the most powerful tools in his kit.
💡 Pull Iron Man out of the sky mid-Unibeam charge to cancel his burst entirely.
SHIFT
Web Swing
Launches a web to a surface and swings toward it at high speed. Primary mobility tool. Can chain multiple swings for near-infinite traversal.
💡 Aim at ceilings for maximum swing arc and speed. Low swings are slower and more predictable.
E
Web Cocoon
Wraps a target in webbing, immobilizing them for 1.5 seconds. Hard CC — sets up kills instantly. Short range, requires close proximity.
💡 Use this immediately after landing from a Web Swing dive. The immobilize window is your burst window.
Q — ULT
Amazing Spider-Man
Spider-Man enters an enhanced state with increased damage, faster web swing speed, and reduced ability cooldowns. Lasts 8 seconds. Transforms him into an unkillable assassin during its duration.
💡 Save it for when enemy supports are isolated or when you need to clean up a teamfight. Don't waste it on full-HP tanks.

Advanced Techniques

Combo Guide

Choose your starting point based on your current skill level:

Standard Dive Combo

  • 1
    Approach from above SHIFT 0.0s
    Use Web Swing to gain height and approach from ceiling angle. Never dive straight in from ground level.
  • 2
    Web Pull to close gap RMB ~0.3s
    Pull the target toward you to cancel any ability they're casting and close the final distance instantly.
  • 3
    Web Cocoon immediately E ~0.5s
    Immobilize the target the moment they're in range. This is your kill window — don't delay.
  • 4
    Burst with Web Shots LMB 0.5–2.0s
    Unload Web Shots during the 1.5s immobilize window. Aim for headshots. Most supports die here.
  • 5
    Escape immediately SHIFT <2.0s
    Don't overstay. The moment the target is down or the cocoon breaks, Web Swing out to safety.
Pro tip: The entire combo should take under 2 seconds. If you're spending more than 3 seconds in the backline, you're overcommitting and will die.

Ultimate Combo

  • 1
    Identify isolated support or low-HP target
    Don't activate the ultimate into a full team — find a 1v1 opportunity first.
  • 2
    Activate Amazing Spider-Man Q
    Pop the ultimate before engaging so the enhanced state is active during your dive.
  • 3
    Chain kills with reduced cooldowns
    Web Cocoon resets faster during the ultimate. Chain dive → kill → dive on the next target within the 8-second window.

Wall Cancel Combo Expert

Exploit Wall Crawl to reset your dive angle mid-approach, making your entry completely unpredictable.

  • 1
    Swing toward a wall at high speed SHIFT
    Aim for a wall adjacent to your target. The wall contact cancels your swing momentum instantly.
  • 2
    Stick to wall, reposition angle PASSIVE
    Use the wall to change your approach vector by 90°. Your target loses visual tracking on you for a critical half-second.
  • 3
    Launch second swing from new angle SHIFT
    Swing off the wall directly onto the target from an unexpected direction. Immediately chain into Web Pull → Cocoon → burst.
Why it works: Most players track Spider-Man's swing arc and pre-aim the exit point. The wall cancel breaks that prediction entirely — they're aiming at where you were, not where you'll be.

Web Pull is for closing distance and interrupting abilities — use it to cancel Iron Man's Unibeam or pull a fleeing support into range. Web Cocoon is your kill setup. The sequence is always: Pull to get in range, then Cocoon to lock them down for the burst.

Never stay on the ground. Always be swinging or on a wall. The moment you stop moving, you're a 250 HP target that dies in 2 seconds. Mobility is your defense — if your Web Swing is on cooldown, you're in danger. Always plan your escape route before diving.
