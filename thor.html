❓ FAQ

Is Thor a tank or DPS?

He's a brawler tank — he deals significant damage while absorbing hits. Play him aggressively, not passively.

How do I deal with Iron Man countering me?

Save Lightning Strike to zone him away from high ground. Focus other targets and let your ranged DPS handle aerial threats.

When is Ragnarok most effective?

On objective points where enemies must stay to contest. They either take the damage or give up the objective.
