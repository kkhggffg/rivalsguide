Thor
Complete Guide

Thor Odinson — the God of Thunder and the most flexible Vanguard in Marvel Rivals. He can frontline, dive backlines, and peel for supports. The hardest part isn't learning his kit — it's knowing which role to play in any given fight.

If you want a tank who can genuinely do everything — brawl other tanks, delete backlines with Awakening, and peel for your supports — Thor is the most rewarding Vanguard to master.

Kit Breakdown

Abilities & Skills

PASSIVE
Mjölnir Throw (Passive Mechanic)
Thor's hammer Mjölnir is the foundation of his entire kit. His primary attacks, Storm Surge mobility, and Awakening state all revolve around Mjölnir — understanding how it interacts with each ability is the core skill of playing Thor. [[0]](#__0)
💡 Thor's kit is built around one principle: use Storm Surge to stay mobile and survive, use Awakening to deal burst damage when it's safe to stand still.
LMB
Storm Surge (Primary Attack)
Type: Melee / Projectile
Range: Medium
Effect: Knockback
Thor swings Mjölnir to strike nearby enemies or launches a storm projectile at range. Storm Surge is also Thor's primary mobility tool — surging in a direction launches him through the air, letting him reposition rapidly, escape danger, or engage from unexpected angles. [[0]](#__0)
💡 Storm Surge is your escape hatch — the moment you're being focused at low HP, surge to cover, high ground, or a health pack. Don't stay in a bad position hoping your healers save you. [[2]](#__2)
F — AWAKEN
Awakening (Mjölnir Empowered)
Bonus HP: +200
Projectile: Large AoE
Damage: DPS-level
Thor channels Mjölnir to enter an empowered state, gaining 200 bonus HP and transforming his attacks into large, high-damage lightning projectiles. The projectiles are large and relatively easy to hit — capable of deleting squishies before their healers can react. However, Thor becomes a large, slow target during Awakening, making him extremely vulnerable to burst DPS and CC. [[2]](#__2)
⚠️ Critical Decision Point: Only pop Awakening when it's safe. If you're being focused, if Bucky has a hook ready, if you don't have cover — do NOT awaken. One Maximum Pulse or a single CC ability while awakened sends you straight back to respawn. The hardest skill on Thor is knowing when not to awaken. [[2]](#__2)
💡 Rotate to an off-angle or high ground BEFORE awakening. Hitting their backline from an unexpected position means they can't immediately focus you down during your vulnerable window.
SHIFT
Fearless Charge / Surge Leap
Type: Dash / Leap
Effect: Gap Close
Thor charges forward with Mjölnir raised, closing distance rapidly on a target or leaping to high ground. Primary engagement tool for dive playstyle — drop from high ground or cover rather than surging in full view of the enemy team. [[2]](#__2)
💡 Never surge into the backline in full view of the entire enemy team. Drop from high ground or approach from cover — the element of surprise is what makes Thor's dive effective. [[2]](#__2)
E
Lightning Realm
Type: AoE Zone
Effect: Slow + Damage
Duration: ~4s
Thor summons a lightning field at a target location that slows and damages enemies within it. Excellent for area denial, disrupting enemy formations, and peeling — dropping it around a dive hero attacking your supports forces them to either take damage and slow, or disengage entirely. Also destroys deployables efficiently. [[0]](#__0)
💡 Drop Lightning Realm around enemies you're fighting in melee — the slow prevents them from escaping your follow-up attacks. Also use it on Peni's nest, Groot walls, and other deployables to clear them instantly.
RMB
Mjölnir Toss
Type: Ranged Projectile
Effect: Stun on hit
Auto-return: Yes
Thor throws Mjölnir at a target, which stuns on hit and automatically returns to his hand. The stun is brief but opens a free combo window. Excellent for interrupting enemy abilities, stunning supports mid-heal, or setting up a kill with Awakening follow-up. [[0]](#__0)
💡 Mjölnir Toss stun into Awakening burst is Thor's highest single-target damage combo. The stun prevents the target from dodging your Awakening projectiles — use it on isolated, high-value targets like enemy supports.
Q — ULT
God of Thunder
Type: AoE Burst
Effect: Massive Lightning Strike
Charge: Fast (Awakening farms)
Thor calls down a massive lightning strike on a targeted area, dealing enormous AoE damage to all enemies caught within it. One of the fastest-charging ultimates in the game when Thor actively uses Awakening — the projectile hits farm ult charge rapidly. Best used on grouped enemies during a teamfight push. [[0]](#__0)
💡 God of Thunder charges fastest when you're consistently landing Awakening hits. Prioritize Awakening uptime not just for damage — every hit accelerates your ultimate. In a coordinated push, combine with Captain America's Freedom Charge for a near-unkillable teamfight window. [[2]](#__2)
Role Guide

Thor's Three Playstyles

Thor is unique among Vanguards — he has three distinct roles and the skill ceiling comes from reading the game and switching between them mid-match. Here's when to use each. [[2]](#__2)

🎯
Ranged DPS (Awakening Bot)
Spam Awakening from cover or high ground. Safe, consistent damage. Best when the enemy has tank-busters or heavy CC that punishes diving. Farm ult charge aggressively. [[2]](#__2)
🪂
Dive Tank
Drop from high ground into the backline. Bully supports and uncontested DPS. Use Storm Surge to escape before the enemy responds. Only stay briefly — get in, disrupt, get out. [[2]](#__2)
🛡
Peel / Protector
Stay near your supports and punish dive heroes. Awakening forces dive tanks to retreat immediately. Lightning Realm slows enemies attacking your backline. Use when your other tank is diving. [[2]](#__2)

Advanced Techniques

Combo Guide

Thor's combos are built around safe Awakening windows and the Mjölnir Toss stun setup. The hardest skill isn't execution — it's reading when it's safe to stand still and awaken. [[2]](#__2)

ComboDifficultyBest For
Safe Awakening Poke ⭐ Easy New Thor players
Toss → Awakening Burst ⭐⭐ Medium Players with basic kit knowledge
High Ground Dive Combo ⭐⭐⭐ Hard Ranked / competitive play
Ultimate Teamfight Combo ⭐⭐⭐⭐ Expert High-rank coordinated play

Safe Awakening Poke

  • 1
    Find cover or off-angle position 0.0s
    Move to a position with cover nearby — high ground, a wall corner, or a flank angle. Never awaken in the open middle of the battlefield where all five enemies can focus you. [[2]](#__2)
  • 2
    Check for CC threats 0.0–1.0s
    Scan for Mantis sleep, Luna freeze, Peni web, or Bucky hook. If any of these are off cooldown and aimed at you, do NOT awaken. Wait for them to waste their ability first. [[2]](#__2)
  • 3
    Activate Awakening F ~1.0s
    Pop Awakening from your safe position. The +200 bonus HP activates immediately — you're now at 800 effective HP and firing DPS-level projectiles. [[0]](#__0)
  • 4
    Target backline heroes LMB 1.0–4.0s
    Aim at supports or low-HP DPS — not tanks. Hitting tanks wastes your Awakening window and farms enemy support ult charge. Prioritize the enemy's healers. [[2]](#__2)
  • 5
    Escape via Storm Surge if focused SHIFT Any
    The moment you're being focus-fired, surge to cover or high ground. Don't stay in Awakening hoping the bonus HP saves you — it won't against coordinated burst. [[2]](#__2)
Core principle: Awakening is not a "press and hold" ability — it's a situational burst window. Pop it when safe, escape when focused. The players who die on Thor are the ones who stay in Awakening too long. [[2]](#__2)

Mjölnir Toss → Awakening Burst

Thor's highest single-target damage combo — the stun removes the target's ability to dodge Awakening projectiles. [[0]](#__0)

  • 1
    Identify isolated high-value target 0.0s
    Look for a support or low-HP DPS separated from their team. The stun combo is wasted on tanks — their HP pool absorbs it. [[0]](#__0)
  • 2
    Throw Mjölnir to stun RMB ~0.3s
    Land the Mjölnir Toss on the target. The stun is brief — you must immediately follow up. Mjölnir auto-returns to your hand after the hit. [[0]](#__0)
  • 3
    Activate Awakening immediately F ~0.5s
    Pop Awakening the moment Mjölnir returns. The target is stunned and cannot dodge — your Awakening projectiles are guaranteed to hit. [[2]](#__2)
  • 4
    Unload Awakening projectiles LMB 0.5–2.5s
    Fire all Awakening shots during the stun window. Most supports die to 2–3 direct hits. The stun window is short — don't hesitate between shots. [[2]](#__2)
  • 5
    Drop Lightning Realm to prevent escape E ~2.5s
    If the target survives and tries to flee, drop Lightning Realm at their feet — the slow prevents escape and the damage finishes them off. [[0]](#__0)
Why it works: The stun removes the target's only defense against Awakening — movement. A stunned support has no way to dodge Thor's large projectiles. This combo can delete a support in under 2 seconds. [[0]](#__0)

High Ground Dive Combo Hard

Thor's dive playstyle — drop from high ground to surprise the backline, disrupt, and escape before the enemy team can respond. [[2]](#__2)

  • 1
    Gain high ground position
    Use Storm Surge to reach elevated terrain above the enemy backline. You want to appear in their vision from above — not charge in from the front where all five enemies can see you coming. [[2]](#__2)
  • 2
    Drop down with Surge Leap SHIFT
    Leap down onto the target support or DPS. The rapid descent gives them minimal reaction time — they're already taking damage before they realize Thor is on them. [[2]](#__2)
  • 3
    Mjölnir Toss on landing RMB
    Immediately throw Mjölnir on landing for the stun. The target is now stunned and you're already in melee range — maximum pressure. [[0]](#__0)
  • 4
    Lightning Realm + Awakening burst E → F → LMB
    Drop Lightning Realm to slow any nearby enemies, then awaken and fire. The slow prevents the target from escaping the stun follow-up and keeps nearby enemies from closing on you. [[2]](#__2)
  • 5
    Escape via Storm Surge before focus fire SHIFT
    After the kill or disruption, immediately surge to cover or back to your team. You've done your job — don't overstay and die to the enemy team's response. [[2]](#__2)
