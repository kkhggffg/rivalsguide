Our Mission

RivalsGuide was created with a simple goal: to help every Marvel Rivals player—from total beginners to ranked competitors—master the game faster and have more fun doing it.

We believe that great strategy shouldn't be locked behind paywalls or buried in scattered forum posts. So we built a centralized hub where you can access hero data, tier lists, combo guides, and AI-powered insights—all in one place, completely free.

Other strategy sites bury you in walls of text or outdated wikis. RivalsGuide is built for fast, visual, and actionable learning:

  • Clean design — no clutter, no popups in your face
  • Mobile-friendly — read guides while queuing on your phone
  • AI-enhanced — personalized advice for your specific matchups
  • Always free — supported by ads, never by paywalls
