Scarlet Witch

⚔️ DPS

Master of Chaos Magic. Scarlet Witch deals sustained AoE damage and excels at disrupting enemy formations with her reality-warping abilities.

⚡ Abilities

🔴 Chaos Bolt (Primary)

Fires continuous chaos energy bolts at enemies. Good sustained damage at medium range with no reload required — keep the pressure on constantly.

🌀 Chaos Control (Shift)

Levitates and repositions quickly. Use to dodge incoming attacks and gain better angles on enemies below you.

💥 Hex Blast (E)

Launches a powerful chaos orb that deals burst damage in an area. Best used on grouped enemies or to finish off low-health targets.

🌌 Reality Erasure (Ultimate)

Channels a massive chaos explosion around her, dealing enormous AoE damage. Devastating against clustered enemies on objectives — one of the highest damage ultimates in the game.

💡 Pro Tips

🎯 Stay Mid-Range

Scarlet Witch's damage falls off at extreme range but she is vulnerable up close. Find the sweet spot at medium distance to maximize output while staying safe from melee heroes.

🌀 Mobility is Survival

Use Chaos Control constantly to avoid being a stationary target. Hovering makes you harder to hit and lets you dodge ultimates from heroes like Thanos or Thor.

💥 Ultimate Combo

Pair Reality Erasure with a Loki clone distraction or Doctor Strange portal to catch enemies grouped in a confined space for maximum wipe potential.

🔴 Prioritize Supports

Your sustained AoE is excellent for pressuring enemy supports who cannot easily dodge. Force their healer to focus on themselves rather than their team.
