Scarlet Witch
Complete Guide

Wanda Maximoff — the Mistress of Chaos Magic who auto-tracks enemies, phases through damage, and erases reality itself with the second-highest damage ultimate in the game.

If you want a Duelist who punishes high-mobility enemies, phases through death twice, and can literally vaporize an entire team with one perfectly timed ultimate — Scarlet Witch is your answer.

Scarlet Witch is one of the most beginner-accessible Duelists in Marvel Rivals thanks to her auto-tracking primary fire — but her skill ceiling lies in knowing when to phase, when to hold your ultimate, and how to position inside her dangerously short 20-meter effective range. In the current meta she sits at C-Tier among Duelists, but in the right hands with the right team composition, her reality-erasing ultimate can single-handedly flip any round.

Kit Breakdown

Abilities & Skills

PASSIVE
Telekinesis
Hold Space while airborne to fall slowly. Wanda descends at a controlled 3.5 m/s instead of dropping freely. Simple passive but critical for extending air time during Mystic Projection, peeking over cover, and dodging ground-level AoE attacks.
💡 Combine with Mystic Projection's free-flight to hover at awkward heights that enemies can't easily track. Most players aim at head level — floating slightly above or below breaks their crosshair placement.
LMB
Chaos Control
Unleashes a continuous stream of Chaos Magic in a cylindrical field — 3m radius, 20m height — dealing 8 base damage per tick plus 0.3% of the target's maximum health per tick, at 10 ticks per second. The auto-tracking locks onto the nearest enemy within the cylinder automatically. Every hit also restores Chaos Energy, which fuels Chthonian Burst charges.
Base DPS: 80/s Bonus: +3% max HP/s Range: 3m radius × 20m Ammo: ∞ Crit: No
💡 Target Vanguards first — the % max HP bonus scales massively against 600–800 HP tanks. A Hulk or Thor takes far more damage per second from Chaos Control than from any other Duelist's primary fire.
RMB
Chthonian Burst
Consumes Chaos Energy to fire explosive magic missiles. Each missile deals 40 direct hit damage plus 40 splash damage in a small AoE at the impact point. Holds up to 4 charges. Charges recharge automatically over time but recharge significantly faster while actively hitting enemies with Chaos Control — making the two abilities a tightly linked loop.
Direct hit: 40 dmg Splash: 40 dmg Max charges: 4 Recharge: faster during Chaos Control
💡 Don't dump all 4 charges at once. Fire 1–2 bursts to spike damage during Chaos Control, then let the charges rebuild while you keep channeling. Full burst dump is best saved for a target that's about to escape.
SHIFT
Mystic Projection
Wanda enters a Phased state — completely invulnerable to all damage — and gains free-flight with a 50% movement speed boost for 1.5 seconds. Has 2 charges with a 10-second cooldown per charge. While phased she appears as a small red mist, which experienced enemies will track and pre-aim. Press Shift again to exit early.
Duration: 1.5s Charges: 2 Cooldown: 10s per charge Speed boost: +50%
💡 Never use both charges back-to-back just to reposition. Always keep at least one charge in reserve as a get-out-of-jail card. If both charges are on cooldown, you are a 250 HP target with no escape — play extremely conservatively.
E
Dark Seal
Fires a chaos orb that creates a static 3m spherical force field on impact with an enemy, terrain, or on manual detonation. Enemies inside the field are periodically stunned every 1.25 seconds for 0.5 seconds each. The field lasts 4.5 seconds. Excellent for zoning choke points, interrupting enemy ability casts, and countering high-mobility heroes who can't easily dodge a lingering AoE.
Field duration: 4.5s Stun interval: 1.25s Stun duration: 0.5s each Cooldown: 12s Radius: 3m sphere
💡 Throw Dark Seal at a choke point before your team pushes through — any enemy holding the angle gets stunned and loses their shot. Also excellent against Black Panther and other high-mobility heroes whose evasive movement can't dodge a lingering stun field.
Q — ULT
Reality Erasure
Wanda charges for 4 seconds — gaining free-flight, 25% movement slow, and 100 bonus HP — then unleashes a devastating 850-damage explosion in a 15-meter spherical radius. Enemies killed by the blast are literally vaporized. This is the second-highest damage ultimate in the entire game after Iron Man's Invincible Pulse Cannon. The catch: the 4-second charge window makes her the most interruptible ultimate in the game — any stun cancels it entirely.
Damage: 850 Radius: 15m sphere Charge time: 4s Bonus HP: 100 Slow: -25% movement
💡 Never charge Reality Erasure in the open. Hide behind cover, use a teammate's shield, or phase into position with Mystic Projection before activating. The best setup is Jeff's cluster ultimate or Doctor Strange's Eye of Agamotto — enemies can't dodge or interrupt if they're already grouped and stunned.
TEAM-UP
Arcane Order — with Doctor Strange
When paired with Doctor Strange, Scarlet Witch gains access to Arcane Order — a team-up ability that combines their magical synergy to enhance crowd control potential. Strange's portal and shield utility directly protects Wanda during her vulnerable 4-second ultimate charge, making this one of the most synergistic pairings in the game.
💡 Have Strange raise his Shield of the Seraphim directly in front of Wanda while she charges Reality Erasure. The shield blocks all incoming projectiles — enemies cannot interrupt the charge unless they commit to a melee dive, which Strange's portal can punish immediately.

Chaos Control Loop

  • 1
    Engage at 15–20m range pre-engage
    Stay at the edge of your effective range — close enough for Chaos Control to auto-track, far enough to have reaction time if dived.
  • 2
    Channel Chaos Control on a Vanguard LMB ongoing
    Lock onto the tankiest enemy. The % max HP damage means you're dealing more effective DPS than any other Duelist against high-HP targets.
  • 3
    Fire 1–2 Chthonian Bursts to spike damage RMB
    Don't dump all 4 charges. Fire 1–2 to spike, then let them recharge while you keep channeling. The loop sustains your burst potential indefinitely.
  • 4
    Phase away if threatened SHIFT
    The moment a diver commits, phase immediately. Reposition to a new angle and resume the loop. Never let both phase charges deplete.
Pro tip: Focus Strategists whenever they're exposed — Wanda's auto-tracking harassment forces supports to constantly reposition, which disrupts the enemy team's healing rotation far more than raw damage numbers suggest.

Dark Seal → Burst Spike

  • 1
    Throw Dark Seal at target's position E 0.0s
    Aim at the ground under a Strategist or low-HP Duelist. The stun field activates on impact and begins pulsing every 1.25 seconds.
  • 2
    Channel Chaos Control immediately LMB ~0.2s
    The stun prevents the target from moving out of your auto-tracking cylinder. You're now dealing full DPS to a stationary target.
  • 3
    Dump all 4 Chthonian Bursts RMB ×4 ~0.5s
    With the target stunned and stationary, unload all 4 burst charges for maximum spike damage. Direct hit + splash on a stunned target is nearly always a kill on any Duelist or Strategist.
Why it works: Dark Seal's stun removes the target's ability to strafe or jump away from Chaos Control's cylinder. Against a stationary target, Wanda's DPS becomes significantly more threatening than her baseline numbers suggest.

Phase-In Reality Erasure

  • 1
    Identify a clustered enemy group from safety
    Never activate Reality Erasure unless 3+ enemies are grouped within 15 meters of your intended detonation point. Patience is the entire skill expression here.
  • 2
    Activate Mystic Projection to fly into position SHIFT
    Phase through any damage during the approach. Land at the center of the enemy cluster — the invulnerability window covers your entire repositioning.
  • 3
    Immediately activate Reality Erasure Q 0.0s
    Pop the ultimate the moment you land in position. Enemies are already in range and have zero time to scatter before the 4-second charge begins.
  • 4
    Fly upward during the charge 0–4s
    Gain height during the charge window. Enemies must now aim upward to interrupt you, and the 15m radius still covers the ground below. Harder to stun from below.
  • 5
    Detonate — save second phase charge for escape SHIFT
    After detonation, immediately phase with your second charge to escape the counterattack. Never use both charges before the ult — always keep one for the getaway.
Why it works: Phasing into position eliminates the most dangerous part of the ultimate — the approach. Enemies can't interrupt what they can't hit. By the time they realize Wanda is in the middle of their team, the charge is already halfway complete.

Strange Shield Ult Cover Expert

Coordinate with Doctor Strange to cover Wanda's entire 4-second charge window — making Reality Erasure nearly impossible to interrupt.

  • 1
    Communicate ult timing with Strange before the fight
    This combo requires voice or text coordination. Strange needs to know exactly when Wanda is activating so he can raise his shield at the right moment.
  • 2
    Wanda activates Reality Erasure from behind Strange Q
    Position directly behind Strange, facing the enemy team. His body and shield create a physical barrier between Wanda and all incoming projectiles.
  • 3
    Strange raises Shield of the Seraphim RMB
    The shield blocks every projectile aimed at Wanda for the full 4-second charge. Enemies must commit to a melee dive to interrupt — which Strange can punish with his own kit.
  • 4
    Detonate — Strange follows up with Eye of Agamotto on survivors
    Any enemies who survived the 850-damage blast are now low HP. Strange pops his ult on the survivors for a near-guaranteed full team wipe.
Why it works: Reality Erasure's only real weakness is the 4-second interrupt window. Strange's shield removes that weakness entirely. This two-hero combination produces one of the highest single-round impact plays in all of Marvel Rivals.

If you're new, she's a great hero to learn game sense on before moving to higher-skill-ceiling picks.
