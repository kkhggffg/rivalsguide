⚡ Abilities

🛡️ Shield Slash (Primary)

Throws his shield in a straight line, dealing damage and bouncing off walls. Great for poking at range and hitting multiple enemies.

💨 Charging Star (Shift)

Dashes forward, stunning the first enemy hit. Core engage tool — use to close gaps and initiate fights on your terms.

🛡️ Vibranium Shield (E)

Raises shield to block incoming projectiles from the front. Essential for protecting yourself and allies during pushes through choke points.

🦅 Freedom Charge (Ultimate)

Charges forward with massive knockback, launching enemies into the air. Excellent for displacing grouped enemies off objectives or into your team's follow-up damage.

💡 Pro Tips

🎯 Shield Cancel Combo

Use Charging Star into melee into Shield Slash for maximum burst damage in close range. Animation canceling is key to high DPS output as Cap.

🛡️ Shield Positioning

Always face your shield toward the highest-damage enemy. Your block only covers the front — positioning and awareness are everything on this hero.

🚀 Ultimate Timing

Save Freedom Charge for when enemies cluster on the point. Launching them off high ground or into your team's follow-up damage is devastating and can wipe entire teams.

🤝 Play With Your Team

Captain America is not a solo carry. His value multiplies when he has a healer behind him and DPS ready to follow up his stuns and knockbacks.
