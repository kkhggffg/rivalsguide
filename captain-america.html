Captain America
Complete Guide

Steve Rogers — the Super-Soldier who doesn't just hold the frontline, he crashes through it. Cap's true power isn't blocking damage — it's terrorizing the enemy backline while his team follows up.

If you want a tank who ignores enemy frontlines, dives enemy supports, and wins teamfights with one of the strongest ultimates in the game — Captain America is your pick.

Captain America is a unique Vanguard — he has 650 HP and a 350-durability Vibranium shield, but his role is NOT to sit at the frontline and absorb damage. Instead, he uses his sprint, leap, and slam to bypass enemy tanks entirely and harass the backline. His damage is low, but his disruption, survivability, and Freedom Charge ultimate make him one of the most impactful tanks in the game.

✅ Strengths
Backline dive disruption · Shield reflects projectiles & CC · Freedom Charge is near-unkillable for the team · CC-immune during Leading Dash
❌ Weaknesses
Very low damage output · Can't frontline vs. other tanks · Extremely long ultimate charge time · Difficult animation cancel execution
🎯 Primary Role
Dive the enemy backline. Ignore their tanks. Force supports to fight you instead of healing their team — that's your entire job.
🛡 Secondary Role
Shield key projectiles and CC abilities during your team's approach. A well-timed reflect on Mantis sleep or Groot spore bomb wins fights.
Kit Breakdown

Abilities & Skills

PASSIVE
Leading Dash (Sprint)
Cap automatically sprints when moving forward, granting increased movement speed and CC immunity during the dash. This is his primary mobility and engagement tool — and critically, he becomes completely immune to crowd control while dashing, letting him run through Luna Snow freezes and other stuns that stop other tanks cold.
💡 Toggle this ON at the start of every match — it turns off when you die, so re-enable it after each respawn. There is no reason to ever have it off.
LMB
Sentinel Strike
Melee DMG: 40
Shield DMG: 45
Range: 4m
Shield Speed: 60m/s
Max Distance: 25m
Bounces: 4x
Two melee strikes followed by a shield throw that ricochets up to 4 times between nearby enemies. The shield auto-tracks the closest enemy to your crosshair, making it reliable against fast-moving targets. Deployables (Peni's nest, Groot walls, Loki clones) count as valid hits to trigger the shield throw.
💡 Animation Cancel: After each shield throw, immediately tap SHIFT (Leading Dash) to cancel the recovery animation, then throw again. This nearly doubles your DPS. Input: LMB → SHIFT → release → LMB → SHIFT → release (repeat for all 4 throws).
AIR LMB
Super-Soldier Slam
DMG: 30
Cooldown: 12s
H. Range: 20m
While airborne, Cap slams down onto a targeted area, launching enemies upward. Your primary engagement tool — dive from high ground or flanks to crash into the backline with knockup. Can also be chained mid-combo (sprint → jump → slam) to chase fleeing targets.
💡 Use slam as your opener when diving supports — the knockup briefly interrupts their healing and gives you free hits. Aim slightly ahead of moving targets; the slam has strong forward momentum.
RMB
Living Legend (Shield Block)
Shield HP: 350
Regen: 50/s
Regen Delay: 4s
Cooldown: 1s
Raises the Vibranium shield to deflect incoming projectiles, sending them ricocheting in random directions at 50% reduced damage. The shield has 350 durability — if depleted, it goes on a 5-second cooldown. Cap is the only dive Vanguard who can rush a support with a stun ability (like Luna Snow) and not get CC'd during his approach.
💡 Shield unpredictably and frequently against enemies with projectile cooldowns like Mantis sleep or Groot's Spore Bomb — they'll waste their abilities trying to time around your shield.
SHIFT
Liberty Rush
A quick forward dash with shield raised. Low damage but excellent for maintaining pressure on retreating enemies and closing the final gap. If you've played Marvel fighting games, this is "Charging Star." Best used to stick to a target that's trying to escape after your slam opener.
💡 Liberty Rush is also your animation cancel tool for Sentinel Strike — use it between shield throws to eliminate recovery frames and dramatically increase your throw frequency.
E
Vibranium Energy Saw
Cap throws his shield at long range with strong auto-aim, bouncing between nearby enemies. Lower damage than Sentinel Strike throws, but usable at full range — making it Cap's only ranged poke tool. Best used to secure kills on fleeing low-HP enemies who've escaped melee range.
💡 Save Vibranium Energy Saw for last-hitting escaping enemies — don't use it as an opener. Its auto-aim makes it reliable at range, but its damage doesn't justify wasting it early in an engagement.
Q — ULT
Freedom Charge
Cap grants himself and all nearby allies 250 bonus health that actively regenerates, plus a movement speed boost. The bonus health decays after the duration ends. This is widely considered one of the strongest ultimates in the game — it makes your entire team nearly unkillable for its duration. Stacked with support ultimates like Luna Snow or Cloak & Dagger, your team can have 20+ seconds of near-invincibility. The major downside: it has an extremely long charge time that doesn't accelerate from kills.
💡 Communicate before using Freedom Charge — notify your team so they push aggressively during the window. Wasted if your team is scattered. Combine with Luna Snow's ult for the most oppressive sustained fight in the game.

Advanced Techniques

Combo Guide

Cap's combos are built around animation cancels and dive sequencing. The shield throw cancel is the single most important mechanical skill to master — it nearly doubles your DPS output.

Basic Dive & Slam

  • 1
    Enable Leading Dash SHIFT 0.0s
    Toggle sprint on at match start. Confirm the icon is active — it turns off on death and must be re-enabled every respawn.
  • 2
    Identify isolated support 0.0–3.0s
    Look for a support separated from their tanks — Mantis, Adam Warlock, or Luna Snow. Never open on a tank; your damage is too low to threaten them.
  • 3
    Approach from high ground + Slam AIR LMB 3.0–4.0s
    Sprint → Fearless Leap → Super-Soldier Slam onto the target. The knockup interrupts their healing and gives you free hits during their stagger.
  • 4
    Follow up with Sentinel Strike LMB 4.0–6.0s
    Two melee hits + shield throw while the target is staggered. Even without the animation cancel, this deals solid burst during the knockup window.
  • 5
    Liberty Rush to finish or escape SHIFT <7.0s
    If the target is low, Liberty Rush to close and finish. If you're taking heavy focus fire, dash out and retreat to a health pack — Cap has no self-sustain.
Key principle: Cap's goal is NOT to get kills — it's to force supports to deal with him instead of healing their team. Even if you don't get the kill, a support spending 5 seconds running from you is a support not healing. [[3]](#__3)

Shield Animation Cancel

The most important mechanical skill for Cap — cancelling the recovery animation between shield throws nearly doubles your damage output. [[2]](#__2)

  • 1
    Land two melee hits LMB × 2 0.0–0.9s
    The first two hits of Sentinel Strike are melee punches. They must both connect to unlock the shield throw. Get close — range is only 4m.
  • 2
    Throw shield LMB ~0.9s
    The third input launches the shield. It auto-tracks the nearest enemy — reliable even against fast-moving targets.
  • 3
    Immediately tap SHIFT to cancel recovery SHIFT ~1.1s
    The moment the shield leaves your hand, tap SHIFT. This cancels the recovery animation entirely, letting you throw again immediately instead of waiting.
  • 4
    Release SHIFT and throw again LMB ~1.4s
    Release SHIFT briefly, then throw the next shield. Repeat the cycle: LMB → SHIFT → release → LMB → SHIFT → release for all 4 throws.
  • 5
    Complete all 4 throws in rapid succession <3.0s
    With the cancel, all 4 shield throws complete in under 3 seconds vs. 5+ seconds without it. This is the difference between Cap being a nuisance and a genuine threat.
Practice note: This cancel requires consistent timing — spend 10 minutes in the practice range before taking it into ranked. The rhythm is: throw → cancel → throw → cancel. Once it clicks, it becomes muscle memory. [[2]](#__2)

Full Backline Dive Combo Hard

Combines the slam opener with the full animation cancel chain — Cap's maximum damage output sequence.

  • 1
    Sprint → Fearless Leap → Slam SHIFT → SPACE → AIR LMB
    Engage from high ground or a flank. The slam knockup is your opener — it gives you a free combo window while the target is in the air.
  • 2
    Two melee hits immediately on landing LMB × 2
    Land the two melee hits the moment you touch down. The target is still in the knockup animation — they can't dodge.
  • 3
    Full 4-throw animation cancel chain LMB → SHIFT × 4
    Execute all 4 shield throws with the SHIFT cancel between each. This is the highest DPS window of the combo — the target is grounded and taking full hits.
  • 4
    Liberty Rush + Vibranium Saw to finish SHIFT → E
    If the target is still alive and fleeing, Liberty Rush to close distance and throw Vibranium Energy Saw for the last hit — its auto-aim makes it reliable at range.

Freedom Charge Teamfight Expert

Coordinating Freedom Charge with support ultimates is Cap's highest-impact play — and the hardest to execute well. [[1]](#__1)

  • 1
    Communicate ult timing with supports
    Call out "Freedom Charge ready" in voice or ping. Ask your Luna Snow or Cloak & Dagger to hold their ult. The combo only works if both ultimates overlap.
  • 2
    Wait for a full teamfight engagement
    Don't pop Freedom Charge preemptively. Wait until both teams are fully committed to the fight — the 250 bonus HP is wasted if your team isn't pushing.
  • 3
    Activate Freedom Charge Q
    Pop it when your team is grouped within range. All nearby allies gain 250 regenerating bonus HP + movement boost. Signal your support to activate their ult simultaneously.
  • 4
    Push aggressively for the full duration
    Your team is near-unkillable for 8–20 seconds depending on support ult overlap. This is the window to capture objectives, push payload, or wipe the enemy team. Don't play passively.
Why it's expert-level: Freedom Charge has the longest charge time of any ult in the game — you may only get it once per match. Wasting it on a disorganized fight is a match-losing mistake. The payoff when executed correctly is unmatched. [[3]](#__3)
