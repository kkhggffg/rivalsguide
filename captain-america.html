⚡ Abilities

🛡️ Shield Slash (Primary)

Throws his shield in a straight line, dealing damage and bouncing off walls. Great for poking at range.

💨 Charging Star (Shift)

Dashes forward, stunning the first enemy hit. Core engage tool — use to close gaps and initiate fights.

🛡️ Vibranium Shield (E)

Raises shield to block incoming projectiles from the front. Essential for protecting yourself and allies during pushes.

🦅 Freedom Charge (Ultimate)

Charges forward with massive knockback, launching enemies into the air. Excellent for displacing grouped enemies off objectives.

💡 Pro Tips

🎯 Shield Cancel Combo

Use Charging Star → melee → Shield Slash for maximum burst damage in close range. Animation canceling is key to high DPS output. [[1]](#__1)

🛡️ Shield Positioning

Always face your shield toward the highest-damage enemy. Your block only covers the front — positioning is everything. [[2]](#__2)

🚀 Ultimate Timing

Save Freedom Charge for when enemies cluster on the point. Launching them off high ground or into your team's follow-up is devastating. [[1]](#__1)
