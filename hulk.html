Leaps a massive distance, landing with an AoE shockwave. Primary engage and escape tool.

❓ FAQ

Is Hulk hard to play?

No — he's one of the most beginner-friendly tanks. Focus on jumping into fights and keeping enemy attention off your team.

When should I use Worldbreaker?

Use it when the enemy team is grouped on an objective or when your team needs to force a fight. Don't waste it on single targets.

How do I deal with ranged counters?

Use terrain as cover while closing the gap. Hulk Leap ignores most obstacles — use it aggressively.
