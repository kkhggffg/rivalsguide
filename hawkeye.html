⚡ Abilities

LMBQuick Shot

Fires a rapid arrow with low charge time. Consistent poke damage at any range. Low cooldown.

RMBCharged Arrow

Hold to fully charge a devastating single arrow. Full charge deals massive damage and can one-shot squishy targets.

SHIFTTrick Arrow

Fires a special arrow that explodes on impact, applying a slow to all enemies in the blast radius for 2s.

EGrapple Line

Fires a grapple to quickly reposition to high ground or escape danger. Essential mobility tool.

QRain of Arrows (Ultimate)

Fires a volley of arrows in a wide arc, raining down on a large area for sustained AoE damage over 4s.

❓ FAQ

Is Hawkeye hard to play?

Moderate — his kit is simple but landing charged shots requires good aim and positioning discipline.

When should I use Charged Arrow vs Quick Shot?

Use Charged Arrow on isolated or slowed targets. Quick Shot is for poke and finishing low-HP enemies.

How do I survive dive assassins?

Always keep Grapple Line ready. The moment a diver closes in, grapple to high ground and reset the fight.
