⚡ Abilities

LMBDual Blasters

Fires both Element Guns simultaneously. High fire rate with moderate damage per shot. Best at close-to-mid range.

RMBCharged Blast

Charges both guns for a powerful dual-shot burst. Deals bonus damage and applies a brief slow on hit.

SHIFTRocket Boots

Activates jet boots for a quick aerial dash. Can be used twice before going on cooldown. Essential for dodging and repositioning.

EGravity Mine

Throws a mine that detonates on contact, pulling nearby enemies toward the center and dealing AoE damage.

QGuardians Unite (Ultimate)

Calls in the Guardians for a coordinated strike, dealing massive AoE damage across a wide area. Also boosts nearby ally damage by 20% for 6s.

❓ FAQ

Is Star-Lord good for beginners?

Yes — his dual blasters are easy to use and Rocket Boots make him forgiving to play. Good for learning aerial combat.

When should I use Guardians Unite?

Use it when enemies are grouped and your team needs a damage boost. The ally buff is often more valuable than the damage itself.

How do I deal with dive assassins?

Use Rocket Boots immediately to create vertical distance. Most melee heroes can't follow you into the air.
