Doctor Strange

Sorcerer Supreme

The first hero added to Marvel Rivals' roster. Doctor Strange commands the frontline with his massive shield, game-changing portals, and a fight-winning ultimate that rips enemy souls from their bodies. Master his Dark Magic mechanic and you'll dictate where every team fight happens.

Abilities

💜
Price of Magic
PASSIVE
Passive

Each hit from primary fire or melee builds Dark Magic stacks (max 100). If you reach 100 and don't release within 3 seconds, you're afflicted with Anti-Heal — preventing all healing for a few seconds. Dark Magic decays naturally when you stop dealing damage.

Dagger hit +3.5 Dark Magic
Melee hit +10 Dark Magic
Cap 100 stacks
Penalty Anti-Heal
  • Weaving melee between shots builds Dark Magic much faster than primary fire alone
  • Once decay starts, you can't regain stacks until fully released or zeroed out
  • Watch the glowing purple cape symbol — brighter means closer to 100
🗡
Daggers of Denak
PRIMARY FIRE · Left Click / R2
Primary

Fires magical daggers forward as delayed-hit projectiles. Slow fire rate but can be weaved with melee to cancel end-lag and boost DPS. Effective against airborne targets like Iron Man, but struggles in close-range brawls against melee heroes.

Damage 16/shot
Magazine 10 shots
Fire Rate 5.56 rps
Speed 60 m/s
Falloff starts 10m
Max falloff 80% @ 20m
  • 0.03s gap between every two rounds — plenty of time to weave in melee or shield
  • Use Shield of the Seraphim to cancel the reload animation, saving ~1/3 of reload time
  • Alternating primary fire and melee is the core DPS technique for Strange
🛡
Shield of the Seraphim
SECONDARY FIRE · Right Click / L2
Defense

Projects a large radial shield in front of Strange with its own health pool, absorbing all incoming projectiles. Movement speed is reduced while active, but it can be used simultaneously while flying. This is Strange's most defining ability — it dictates the entire team's positioning.

  • Blocks projectile ultimates like Hela's Goddess of Death and Iron Man's ult
  • Cannot block melee attacks — Wolverine, Hulk, and Iron Fist punch straight through
  • Play passively near cover when shield HP is low; push aggressively when it's full
  • Cancel the primary fire reload with shield to save ~1/3 of reload time
  • Holding shield while flying gives both height advantage and projectile protection
🌀
Maelstrom of Madness
ABILITY 1 · Right Click / R1
Burst

Releases all accumulated Dark Magic as an AoE burst around Strange. Damage scales directly with current Dark Magic stacks at 1.2 damage per stack, dealing up to 120 damage at full charge. Also clears Dark Magic to prevent the Anti-Heal curse.

Formula Stacks × 1.2
Max Damage 120
Type Melee-range AoE
  • Always release as close to 100 stacks as possible to maximize damage
  • Requires melee range — use your shield to close the gap safely
  • Teaming with Hulk triggers Gamma Maelstrom, significantly boosting this ability's damage
  • Can be used defensively just to dump stacks and avoid the Anti-Heal penalty
🌐
Pentagram of Farallah
ABILITY 2 · Triangle / Y
Tactical

Opens two portals anywhere on the map, lasting 20 seconds. Both allies and enemies can pass through, and all projectiles — including ultimates — travel through the portals. The first portal's position can be repositioned before placing the second, offering massive tactical flexibility.

Duration 20s
Cooldown Very Long
Projectiles Pass Through
  • Any hit during the cast instantly cancels it and triggers the full cooldown — cast from safety
  • Casting mid-air causes Strange to hover until the second portal is placed
  • Projectiles can only pass through once per cast — no bouncing tricks
  • Best use: teleport your team from spawn to the objective, or flank behind the enemy backline
  • Practice portal placement in the Practice Range — every second of execution time matters
✈
Cloak of Levitation
MOVEMENT · Shift / L1
Mobility

Strange takes flight, allowing aerial attacks or escapes from danger. The cast has a notable wind-up during which Strange is fully exposed. Flight speed is not fast enough to reliably escape aggressive melee divers.

  • Immediately fly when Hulk or Wolverine dive you — you cannot win that ground fight
  • Use to fly over terrain obstacles like Groot's walls
  • Escape Jeff the Land Shark's swallow ultimate by going airborne
  • Flying + shield simultaneously gives both height advantage and projectile defense
  • Drop from high ground or flank angles before using your ultimate for maximum surprise
👁
Eye of Agamotto
ULTIMATE · Q / Both Sticks
Ultimate

Releases a shockwave in a 20-meter diameter around Strange, ripping the souls of all enemies within range from their bodies for 3 seconds. All damage dealt to the projected souls is reflected back to their physical bodies. Even in a losing fight, one well-placed ult can instantly flip the round.

Range 20m diameter
Stun Duration 3 seconds
Soul Damage 100% reflected
  • Use when enemies are grouped — the more souls caught, the higher the value
  • Dive from high ground or flanks to reduce the chance of enemies dodging out of range
  • If Strange is walking past your tanks, he's likely looking for an ult angle — spread out
  • Your team must follow up immediately — the 3-second window is everything
  • Weaving primary fire and melee charges the ult meter faster
🤝
Gamma Maelstrom
TEAM-UP · With Hulk
Team-Up

When teamed with Hulk, Doctor Strange receives excess Gamma energy, significantly boosting the damage of Maelstrom of Madness. Strange also has team-up synergies with Scarlet Witch and Magik.

  • Hulk + Strange is a classic high-pressure dive composition
  • Scarlet Witch and Magik synergies enhance magical crowd control potential

🧠 Strategy Guide

🛡 Shield Management
  • ▸ Your shield health is your aggression meter — full shield means push forward, low shield means fall back
  • ▸ Face the largest source of incoming damage — the shield only covers the front
  • ▸ Raise shield proactively when you see enemy ult animations charging up
  • ▸ Don't hold shield permanently — it slows you and you need to deal damage to charge Dark Magic
🌀 Portal Usage
  • ▸ Use at the start of rounds to teleport your whole team to the objective instantly
