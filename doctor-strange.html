⚡ Abilities

LMBMystic Bolt

Fires bolts of sorcerous energy. Reliable mid-range damage with decent projectile speed.

RMBShield of the Seraphim

Conjures a magical barrier that absorbs incoming damage. The most important ability — timing is everything.

SHIFTLevitation

Rises into the air for repositioning. Allows Strange to access elevated positions and dodge ground-level attacks.

ESling Ring Portal

Opens a portal that can teleport Strange or allies. Exceptional for repositioning the team mid-fight.

QEye of Agamotto (Ultimate)

Releases a powerful time-distortion field that slows and damages all enemies caught within it. Game-changing teamfight tool.

❓ FAQ

Is Doctor Strange a tank or mage?

He plays as a utility tank — his value comes from shield timing and portal plays, not raw damage output.

When should I use the portal?

Use it to save a dying teammate, to flank before your ultimate, or to escape a losing fight.

How do I time the shield correctly?

Watch for wind-up animations on big abilities (Unibeam, Thor's hammer throw). Pre-casting slightly early is better than reacting late.
