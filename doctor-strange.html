Doctor Strange
Complete Guide

Stephen Strange — the Sorcerer Supreme who bends space, shields his team from devastation, and rips enemy souls from their bodies with a single ultimate.

If you want to dictate where every team fight happens and hold the power to flip a losing round in 3 seconds — Doctor Strange is your answer.

Doctor Strange is the first hero added to Marvel Rivals and one of the most tactically deep Vanguards in the roster. His massive shield controls entire sightlines, his portals rewrite team positioning in an instant, and his ultimate can stun every enemy in a 20-meter radius. The key to unlocking his full potential is mastering the Dark Magic mechanic — build stacks fast, release at the right moment, and never let the Anti-Heal curse trigger on you.

Kit Breakdown

Abilities & Skills

PASSIVE
Price of Magic
Every hit from primary fire or melee builds Dark Magic stacks up to 100. Reaching the cap and failing to release within 3 seconds triggers Anti-Heal — preventing all healing. Stacks decay naturally when you stop dealing damage.
Dagger hit: +3.5 stacks Melee hit: +10 stacks Cap: 100 stacks
💡 Melee gives nearly 3× more Dark Magic per hit than daggers. Weaving melee between shots is the fastest way to charge Maelstrom of Madness.
LMB
Daggers of Denak
Fires five-round delayed-hit projectiles forward. Slow fire rate with a 0.03s gap between every two rounds — but that gap is your window to weave in melee or shield to cancel end-lag and boost DPS. Effective against airborne targets; struggles in close-range brawls.
Damage: 16/shot Magazine: 10 shots Speed: 60 m/s Falloff: starts at 10m
💡 Raise Shield of the Seraphim during reload to cancel ~1/3 of the reload animation. This adds up significantly over a full match.
RMB
Shield of the Seraphim
Projects a large radial shield in front of Strange with its own health pool, absorbing all incoming projectiles. Movement speed is reduced while active. Can be used simultaneously while flying. This is Strange's most defining ability — it controls where the entire team fights.
💡 The shield blocks projectile ultimates like Hela's and Iron Man's — but melee attacks punch straight through. If Wolverine or Hulk dives you, fly away immediately.
R1 / RB
Maelstrom of Madness
Releases all accumulated Dark Magic as a melee-range AoE burst. Damage scales at 1.2 per stack — meaning a full 100-stack release deals 120 damage to everything around you. Also clears your stacks to prevent the Anti-Heal curse.
Formula: stacks × 1.2 Max damage: 120 Type: melee-range AoE
💡 Always release as close to 100 stacks as possible. Teaming with Hulk triggers Gamma Maelstrom, significantly boosting this ability's damage output.
E / Y
Pentagram of Farallah
Opens two portals anywhere on the map, lasting 20 seconds. Both allies and enemies can pass through, and all projectiles — including ultimates — travel through the portals. The first portal's position can be repositioned before placing the second.
Duration: 20s Cooldown: Very long Projectiles: pass through
💡 Any hit during the cast instantly cancels it and triggers the full cooldown. Always cast from safety. Best use: teleport your whole team from spawn to the objective at round start.
SHIFT / L1
Cloak of Levitation
Strange takes flight, allowing aerial attacks or escapes. The cast has a notable wind-up during which Strange is fully exposed to all attacks. Flight speed is not fast enough to reliably escape aggressive melee divers.
💡 Use it to escape Hulk and Wolverine dives, fly over Groot's walls, or dodge Jeff the Land Shark's swallow ultimate. Flying + shield simultaneously gives both height and projectile defense.
Q — ULT
Eye of Agamotto
Releases a shockwave in a 20-meter diameter around Strange, ripping the souls of all enemies within range from their bodies for 3 seconds. All damage dealt to the projected souls is reflected back to their physical bodies. Even in a losing fight, one well-placed ult can instantly flip the round.
Range: 20m diameter Stun: 3 seconds Soul damage: 100% reflected
💡 Dive from high ground or flank angles to reduce the chance of enemies dodging out of range. Your team must follow up immediately — the 3-second window is everything.
TEAM-UP
Gamma Maelstrom — with Hulk
When teamed with Hulk, Doctor Strange receives excess Gamma energy, significantly boosting the damage of Maelstrom of Madness. Strange also has team-up synergies with Scarlet Witch and Magik that enhance magical crowd control potential.
💡 Hulk + Strange is a classic high-pressure dive composition — Hulk draws all frontline aggro while Strange flanks and unloads Maelstrom from the side.

Advanced Techniques

Combo Guide

Primary / Melee Weave

  • 1
    Fire primary LMB 0.0s
    Fire one dagger. Don't wait for the end-lag animation to finish.
  • 2
    Immediately melee MELEE ~0.1s
    The melee cancels the dagger's end-lag frame. You deal both hits faster than firing two daggers normally.
  • 3
    Repeat the loop ongoing
    Each melee hit gives +10 Dark Magic vs +3.5 from daggers. Dark Magic charges to 100 in roughly half the time.
  • 4
    Release Maelstrom at 100 stacks R1
    Full 120 AoE burst. Most squishies die here. Immediately resume the weave loop to recharge.
Pro tip: This weave also charges your ultimate meter faster. Players who master this loop consistently out-damage Strange players who only use primary fire.

Shield Reload Cancel

  • 1
    Deplete magazine LMB
    Fire until the reload animation begins.
  • 2
    Immediately raise shield RMB ~0.1s
    Activating Shield of the Seraphim interrupts the reload animation mid-cycle.
  • 3
    Lower shield — reload completes ~0.3s saved
    The reload finishes roughly 1/3 faster than normal. Resume firing immediately.
Why it matters: Over a full match this saves several seconds of dead time where Strange can't deal damage or build Dark Magic. Small edge, massive compounding advantage.

Portal Flank + Ult Expert

Use Pentagram of Farallah to appear behind the enemy backline before popping Eye of Agamotto — making the stun nearly impossible to dodge.

  • 1
    Cast portal from a safe off-angle E
    Find cover before casting — any hit cancels the portal and triggers full cooldown. Take your time placing it correctly.
  • 2
    Place exit portal directly behind enemy backline
    Aim for a spot where 3+ enemies are clustered. The portal exit should be within Eye of Agamotto's 20m diameter range.
  • 3
    Step through — enemies are caught completely off guard
    Enemies rarely expect Strange to appear behind them. They're facing the wrong direction.
  • 4
    Immediately pop Eye of Agamotto Q
    Fire the ult the moment you step through. Enemies have zero time to spread out or react.
  • 5
    Your team follows through the portal to clean up
    The portal is still open. Your team pours through and unloads on the stunned souls. 3-second window — communicate before executing.
Why it works: Enemies track Strange's position on the frontline and expect the ult to come from that direction. The portal breaks their spatial awareness entirely — they're pre-aiming the wrong angle when the shockwave hits.
