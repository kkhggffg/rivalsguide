Namor

⚔️ DPS

King of Atlantis. Namor is a zone-control DPS who summons sea creatures to dominate areas and punish enemies who cluster together.

⚡ Abilities

🌊 Trident Surge (Primary)

Fires a burst of water energy from his trident. Consistent medium-range damage with good projectile speed — use to poke enemies out of position.

🦈 Aquatic Summon (Shift)

Summons a sea creature turret that attacks nearby enemies automatically. Place these on flanks and choke points to control space and force enemies to deal with them.

💨 Atlantean Flight (E)

Briefly takes flight, gaining mobility and a better angle on enemies. Use to escape flankers or reposition your turrets to better coverage spots.

🌊 Tidal Wave (Ultimate)

Unleashes a massive wave that deals huge damage and pushes enemies back. Devastating on payload maps and choke points — coordinate with your team to follow up the knockback.

💡 Pro Tips

🦈 Turret Placement is Everything

Place Aquatic Summons on high ground or behind cover where enemies cannot easily destroy them. Well-placed turrets force enemies to deal with them or take constant chip damage throughout the fight.

🌊 Ultimate on Choke Points

Tidal Wave is best used when enemies are funneled through a doorway or narrow corridor. The knockback sends them flying and opens them up for your team's follow-up damage.

📍 Set Up Before the Fight

Rotate early and place turrets before enemies arrive. A fully set-up Namor with three turrets on the objective is extremely difficult to push into without taking heavy damage.

✈️ Use Flight to Escape Flankers

Atlantean Flight is your panic button when a Spider-Man or Star-Lord dives you. Use it immediately to gain height and distance, then let your turrets deal with them.
