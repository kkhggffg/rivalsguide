Namor

⚔️ DPS

King of Atlantis. Namor is a zone-control DPS who summons sea creatures to dominate areas and punish enemies who cluster together. [[1]](#__1)

Health

⚡ Abilities

🌊 Trident Surge (Primary)

Fires a burst of water energy from his trident. Consistent medium-range damage with good projectile speed.

🦈 Aquatic Summon (Shift)

Summons a sea creature turret that attacks nearby enemies. Place these on flanks and choke points to control space. [[1]](#__1)

💨 Atlantean Flight (E)

Briefly takes flight, gaining mobility and a better angle. Use to escape flankers or reposition turrets.

🌊 Tidal Wave (Ultimate)

Unleashes a massive wave that deals huge damage and pushes enemies back. Devastating on payload maps and choke points.

💡 Pro Tips

🦈 Turret Placement is Everything

Place Aquatic Summons on high ground or behind cover. Well-placed turrets force enemies to deal with them or take constant chip damage. [[1]](#__1)

🎯 Counter Him: Stay Mobile

Psylocke is the best flanker against Namor — she has excellent poke range and doesn't need to expose herself to his turrets. [[3]](#__3)

🌊 Ultimate on Chokes

Tidal Wave is best used when enemies are funneled through a doorway or narrow corridor. Coordinate with your team to follow up the knockback. [[2]](#__2)
