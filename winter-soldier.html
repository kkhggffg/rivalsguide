Winter Soldier
Complete Guide

Bucky Barnes — the bionic-armed assassin who pulls enemies out of position, chains ultimate resets, and deletes backlines before they can react.

If you want a Duelist who can one-trick from Bronze to GM with explosive burst, team-wiping ultimates, and the single best displacement ability in the game — Winter Soldier is your answer.

Winter Soldier is a medium-range Duelist who excels at both burst and sustained damage — a combination most heroes can't offer. His passive reload mechanic means every ability use also refreshes his gun, so spreading cooldowns correctly keeps him firing constantly. The threat of Bionic Hook alone forces enemies to rethink how they position, making him one of the highest-impact Duelists in the roster even before a single shot is fired.

Kit Breakdown

Abilities & Skills

PASSIVE
Tainted Voltage
Every ability use — Bionic Hook, Trooper's Fist, or Cyro-Grenade — automatically reloads Winter Soldier's Roterstern pistol. This passive is the engine of his entire kit. It means he should never be caught with an empty gun if he manages his cooldowns correctly.
💡 Spread your ability uses across the fight rather than dumping them all at once. Each one is a free reload — staggering them means you're almost always firing a full magazine.
LMB
Roterstern
Fires explosive rounds from his bionic arm blaster. Each shot deals damage to the primary target and splashes to enemies within 5 meters behind them. Three-round magazine with a fast fire rate. The splash damage is not a bonus — it's a core part of his kit that punishes grouped enemies.
Magazine: 3 rounds Splash radius: 5m behind target Type: explosive projectile
💡 Never stand directly behind an ally being shot at — his splash damage hits teammates too. Enemies should avoid clustering for the same reason.
SHIFT
Bionic Hook
Charges up and launches a hook from his bionic arm, reeling in the first target hit plus any enemies lurking directly behind them. The pulled targets land right in front of Winter Soldier — perfectly positioned for a follow-up combo. This is the most impactful ability in his kit and one of the best displacement tools in all of Marvel Rivals.
Type: charge + projectile Pull: primary + enemies behind Triggers: passive reload
💡 After landing Hook, immediately turn and uppercut with Trooper's Fist to launch the pulled enemy into your team. The combo is nearly a guaranteed kill on any Duelist or Strategist.
E
Trooper's Fist
Dashes forward a short distance and delivers a powerful bionic uppercut, launching the target upward. Primary use is as a follow-up to Bionic Hook — the uppercut sends the pulled enemy airborne, giving your team an easy target. Also triggers the passive reload. Despite being labeled a mobility tool, the dash distance is very short.
Type: dash + melee Effect: launches target upward Triggers: passive reload
💡 Don't rely on this as an escape tool — the distance is too short. Its only reliable use is as a follow-up to Hook or as a melee finisher on low-HP targets.
F
Cyro-Grenade
Throws a cryo grenade that slows and briefly immobilizes enemies in its blast radius. Provides a third trigger for the passive reload. Best used to zone enemies away from cover, slow a fleeing target, or set up a Bionic Hook on a group of clustered enemies.
Type: AoE projectile Effect: slow + immobilize Triggers: passive reload
💡 Throw it at the feet of a fleeing support to slow them before launching Bionic Hook. The slow makes the hook almost impossible to dodge.
Q — ULT
Kraken Impact
Winter Soldier leaps high into the air, then dashes forward and slams down with his bionic arm, dealing heavy damage to all enemies in range and marking them for culling. Any marked enemy whose HP drops below a threshold is instantly eliminated — and each kill recharges the bionic arm for another Kraken Impact within a short window. Chain kills correctly and this ultimate never ends.
Type: leap + slam AoE Mark: execute below threshold Reset: each kill = new charge
💡 Don't activate Kraken Impact into a full-health team — combo it with Thor's or Venom's ult first to chunk everyone down, then slam in for the execute chain. The 8-second reset window is long — take your time picking the next target.
TEAM-UP
Supersoldier Tactics — with Captain America
When paired with Captain America, Winter Soldier gains enhanced combat synergy — Cap's shield throw can set up perfect Bionic Hook angles, and the two supersoldiers' coordinated aggression creates a dual frontline threat that is extremely difficult for enemies to manage simultaneously.
💡 Cap charges in and draws attention; Winter Soldier hooks the distracted support from the side. The enemy team is forced to split focus between two high-pressure threats at once.

Advanced Techniques

Combo Guide

Choose your starting point based on your current skill level:

Combo Difficulty Best For
Hook → Uppercut Combo ⭐ Easy New Winter Soldier players
Cryo Slow → Hook Confirm ⭐⭐ Medium Players with Hook timing down
Kraken Impact Execute Chain ⭐⭐⭐ Hard Players ready for ranked carry
Passive Reload Weave ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ Expert High-rank / competitive play

Hook → Uppercut Combo

  • 1
    Identify an isolated target pre-engage
    Look for a support or Duelist separated from their tank. Never Hook into a clustered group — you'll pull multiple enemies and get overwhelmed.
  • 2
    Launch Bionic Hook SHIFT 0.0s
    Charge briefly for better accuracy, then release. The hook pulls the target directly in front of you — and anyone standing behind them.
  • 3
    Immediately Trooper's Fist E ~0.3s
    The moment the target lands in front of you, uppercut them into the air. This launches them toward your team and triggers your passive reload simultaneously.
  • 4
    Burst with Roterstern LMB 0.3–1.5s
    Unload your freshly reloaded magazine into the airborne target. Three explosive rounds into a Duelist or Strategist is almost always a kill.
Pro tip: After the uppercut, turn slightly toward your team before firing — the launched enemy is flying toward them, and your splash damage will hit them from behind for bonus damage.

Cryo Slow → Hook Confirm

  • 1
    Throw Cyro-Grenade at target's feet F 0.0s
    Aim at the ground directly under a mobile target — supports or Duelists who would normally dodge your Hook. The slow makes them nearly stationary.
  • 2
    Launch Bionic Hook into the slow SHIFT ~0.5s
    The slowed target cannot dodge. This is a near-guaranteed Hook hit on any hero in the game, including high-mobility targets like Spider-Man.
  • 3
    Uppercut → burst as normal E → LMB
    Complete the standard Hook → Uppercut → burst sequence. Two passive reloads triggered in quick succession means you have a full magazine for the burst.
Why it works: Bionic Hook's only real weakness is that mobile heroes can sidestep it. Cyro-Grenade removes that weakness entirely — the slow window is long enough to land the Hook at any reasonable range.

Kraken Impact Execute Chain Hard

Combine Kraken Impact with a teammate's chunking ultimate to chain-execute the entire enemy team within the reset window.

  • 1
    Wait for Thor or Venom to pop their ult first
    Thor's Lightning Strike and Venom's ult deal massive damage without eliminating. This is the setup — enemies are chunked to low HP but still alive.
  • 2
    Activate Kraken Impact Q
    Leap up and slam into the largest cluster of chunked enemies. The execute threshold activates on anyone below the HP mark — multiple kills trigger immediately.
  • 3
    Take 8 seconds to select your next target carefully
    The reset window is 8 full seconds — much longer than it feels. Don't panic-activate the next charge. Look for the lowest-HP enemy and dive precisely.
  • 4
    Chain until the team is wiped or the window expires
    Each kill resets the charge. A clean execution on a 5-player team is entirely possible — and it happens regularly at high rank.
Why it works: Most players activate Kraken Impact too early into full-HP enemies and get zero resets. The combo only works when enemies are already chunked. Patience before activation is the entire skill expression of this technique.

Passive Reload Weave Expert

Stagger all three ability cooldowns so that a passive reload triggers every time your magazine empties — achieving near-zero downtime.

  • 1
    Fire 3 rounds → use Cyro-Grenade LMB ×3 → F
    Empty the magazine, then immediately throw the grenade. The passive reload triggers — full magazine again instantly.
  • 2
    Fire 3 rounds → use Trooper's Fist LMB ×3 → E
    Empty again, then dash-uppercut. Second passive reload. You've fired 6 rounds with zero manual reload time.
  • 3
    Fire 3 rounds → use Bionic Hook LMB ×3 → SHIFT
    Third passive reload. You've now fired 9 rounds across the full cooldown cycle with zero manual reload animations.
Why it matters: A Winter Soldier who manually reloads is losing roughly 1.5 seconds of DPS per reload. Over a full fight, the passive weave adds up to several extra magazines of damage — the difference between a kill and a near-miss.
Common Questions

FAQ

He's one of the better Duelists for players who want to one-trick from day one. His kit is straightforward — Hook, uppercut, burst — and his passive reload is forgiving enough that mistakes don't cost you as much as they would on Spider-Man. The main skill curve is Hook accuracy and knowing when to activate Kraken Impact. Spend 15 minutes in the Practice Range landing Hooks on moving targets before your first ranked game.

Hook is best used on isolated Duelists or Strategists — anyone with low HP and no escape tool. The threat of Hook is also a weapon: enemies will back away from good angles just to avoid being pulled, which gives your team free space. Never Hook into a clustered group of 3+ enemies unless you want to pull all of them into your face at once.

Stay close to your Vanguards at all times. Winter Soldier is not a flanker — he's a frontline Duelist who needs a tank to play around. If your tanks are dead or flanking, fall back to cover immediately. Trooper's Fist covers a very short distance, so don't rely on it to escape. Your survival comes from positioning, not movement.

Never activate Kraken Impact into full-HP enemies. Always wait for a teammate's chunking ultimate first — Thor, Venom, or even Scarlet Witch. The execute threshold only triggers when enemies are already low. The 8-second reset window feels short but is actually very generous — take your time selecting the next target instead of panic-activating the next charge.

High-mobility melee divers like Spider-Man and Wolverine are his hardest counters. Spider-Man can dodge Hook almost every time and dives faster than Bucky can respond. Wolverine's regeneration outlasts his burst. If you're facing these heroes, ask your support to peel for you and try to use Cyro-Grenade to slow them before attempting a Hook.
