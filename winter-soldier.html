⚡ Abilities

LMBBionic Strike

Fast 3-hit melee combo with the cybernetic arm. Each hit applies a stacking vulnerability debuff, increasing subsequent damage by 5%.

RMBPistol Burst

Fires a 3-round burst from his sidearm. Effective at close-to-mid range for mixed melee/ranged pressure.

SHIFTGrapple Dash

Fires a grapple hook to dash rapidly to a target location. Can be used mid-air. Core engage and escape tool.

EArm Slam

Slams the bionic arm into the ground, creating a shockwave that knocks up nearby enemies for 0.8s.

QAssassinate (Ultimate)

Dashes to a target enemy and delivers a devastating combo strike, dealing massive single-target damage. Resets on kill.

🎯 Combo Guide

Standard Assassination Combo

1
Use Grapple Dash to close distance on the priority target
2
Immediately land Arm Slam to knock them up and interrupt abilities
3
Follow with full Bionic Strike combo (3 hits) to stack vulnerability
4
Mix in Pistol Burst between melee hits for extra damage
5
If target survives, use Assassinate ultimate to finish and reset for the next target

Chain Kill Combo

1
Activate Assassinate on a low-HP target to secure the kill
2
On reset, immediately Grapple Dash to the next target
3
Repeat — chain kills can wipe an entire backline in seconds

❓ FAQ

Is Winter Soldier good for beginners?

Moderate — his kit is straightforward but timing the Arm Slam knockup into the full combo requires practice.

When should I use Assassinate?

Use it to finish targets below 40% HP to guarantee a kill and reset the cooldown for chain kills.

How do I deal with tanks?

Don't — Winter Soldier is a backline assassin. Focus on supports and DPS, let your tank handle the enemy tanks.
