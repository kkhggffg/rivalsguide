⚡ Abilities

LMBInfinity Gauntlet Strike

Powerful melee strikes with the Infinity Gauntlet. Each hit has a chance to proc a random Infinity Stone effect — from slows to AoE explosions.

RMBReality Warp

Creates a gravity anomaly that pulls nearby enemies toward the center, dealing sustained damage. Excellent for grouping enemies for AoE follow-up.

SHIFTSpace Jump

Teleports instantly to a target location using the Space Stone. Passes through walls. 10s cooldown. Core engage and escape tool.

EMind Control

Takes control of one enemy hero for 2s, forcing them to attack their own team. 15s cooldown. Game-changing CC ability.

QThe Snap (Ultimate)

Deals massive damage to all enemies in sight. Enemies below 30% HP are instantly eliminated. 90s cooldown. One of the most powerful ultimates in the game.

🎯 Combo Guide

1

3

❓ FAQ

Is Thanos good for beginners?

Yes — his high HP makes him forgiving, and his kit is impactful even without perfect execution. Great for learning the game.

When should I use The Snap?

Never use it on full-HP enemies. Wait until your team has poked multiple enemies below 30% for guaranteed multi-kills.

Who should I Mind Control?

Always prioritize the enemy healer or carry DPS. Turning their best player against them is a guaranteed teamfight win.
