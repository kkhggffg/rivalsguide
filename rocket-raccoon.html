Rocket Raccoon

💚 Support

The galaxy's greatest engineer and reluctant hero. Rocket provides healing, utility gadgets, and even a revive mechanic — making him one of the most impactful supports in the game.

⚡ Abilities

🔧 Heal Beam (Primary)

Fires a continuous healing beam at allies. Prioritize your tank and highest-value DPS during fights.

💣 Jetpack Boost (Shift)

Launches Rocket into the air for repositioning. Use to escape flankers and reach safer healing angles.

🔩 Ammo Pack (E)

Throws an ammo/buff pack to an ally, granting them bonus damage or healing. Prioritize DPS allies during their ultimate windows.

🚀 Revive Beacon (Ultimate)

Places a beacon that can revive fallen allies. Timing this correctly after a team fight can completely swing the game. Coordinate with your team before using.

💡 Pro Tips

🔧 Stay Behind Your Tank

Rocket is small and easy to overlook, but flankers will target him. Always keep your tank between you and the enemy frontline.

🚀 Revive Timing

Don't panic-revive immediately after a teammate dies. Wait until the fight calms down, then place the beacon safely. A revived teammate in a bad position just dies again.

🔩 Buff the Right Ally

Give your Ammo Pack to the DPS with the highest kill potential right before a fight. Buffed Star-Lord or Hawkeye can carry entire team fights.
