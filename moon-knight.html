Moon Knight

⚔️ DPS

Avatar of Khonshu. Moon Knight is a unique DPS who excels at bouncing crescent darts off surfaces to hit enemies behind cover — a true skill-expression hero.

⚡ Abilities

🌙 Crescent Dart (Primary)

Throws crescent-shaped projectiles that bounce off walls and surfaces. Mastering bounce angles is the core skill of Moon Knight.

🦅 Cape Glide (Shift)

Glides forward using his cape for repositioning. Use to reach elevated positions or escape flankers.

⚡ Ankh Summon (E)

Places an Ankh totem that amplifies nearby damage. Positioning the Ankh near grouped enemies dramatically increases your team's output.

🌕 Moonfall (Ultimate)

Calls down a massive lunar strike in a large area. Excellent for clearing objectives and punishing tightly grouped enemies.

💡 Pro Tips

🎯 Learn the Bounce Angles

Spend time in the practice range learning how darts bounce off common wall angles. Hitting enemies behind cover is Moon Knight's biggest advantage.

⚡ Ankh Placement

Place your Ankh in high-traffic areas or near the objective. The damage amplification can turn a losing fight into a wipe if positioned correctly.

🌕 Ultimate Combo

Combine Moonfall with Doctor Strange's portal to pull enemies into the strike zone. Coordinate with your team for a guaranteed team wipe.
